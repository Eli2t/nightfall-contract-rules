# Lore

## Basic Lore Overview

### Introduction

This version of the contract takes place in the “Nightfall Cinematic Universe”,
a setting where during the mid 20th century (around the end of World War II),
the existence of the supernatural at-large was discovered.

### World Overview

The Nightfall Cinematic Universe is a version of our modern-day earth where subtle supernatural phenomena have existed as long as life itself. In recent times, the existence of these supernatural occurrences has become more researched and widely known, however very little is understood about the forces behind them. The general population tends to live in fear of these supernatural forces, however there are groups who wish to exploit and utilize these powers.

### The Supernatural

As for the supernatural; supernatural phenomena are exceedingly rare. Generally, unless one actively seeks them, most people encounter the supernatural no more than once within their lifetime, with the chances of being directly affected being even lower. Most sources point to there being no more than 3,000,000 Superabled individuals globally–with the vast majority possessing merely minor supernatural capabilities or capacities.

# A History Of The Supernatural

Our version of the Contract takes place in a setting called the Nightfall
Cinematic Universe where the supernatural is prevalent throughout all of
society.

Grounded in the modern-day Earth where the supernatural is not hidden, but
commodified, militarized, and worshipped, the Nightfall Universe is a fractured,
volatile world governed by government-backed "supes," rogue mystics, and ancient
esoteric powers struggling back into the modern world. Magic is real. Gods are
dying or dead. And power, whether arcane, divine, or genetic always comes at a
cost. The average person has seen the mystic at least once in their life, stigma
towards this is not uncommon and is heavily regulated by government agencies.

Each nation clandestinely oversees and controls the supernatural within its
borders. These organizations consist of black ops agencies and sorcerer
societies which strive to find a balance to the supernatural and keep it from
getting beyond its control.

As wars were waged and revolutions came and went, the supernatural undercurrent
has continually influenced history. The Great Witch Hunts of the 1600s were not
merely religious fervor, they were state-sponsored campaigns to eradicate
powerful magic users who had started to question the status quo.

In the Nightfall Cinematic Universe, the supernatural often leaks onto platforms
like TikTok, but it's heavily manipulated, commercialized, or dismissed. Fakes,
AI and charlatans however are far more common. Unless something is verified by
witnesses, a position of authority or multiple camera angles there is a lot of
room for speculation.

The convergence, estimated by historians to have taken place in the 1st century,
broke the walls between worlds, flooding Earth with supernatural energy. This
was a cataclysmic event which shattered the very fabric of reality. Whatever the
reason, whether a ritual went wrong, a deity died, or a comet arrived from the
cosmos, something brought demons, fae, spirits, and ancient powers into the
mortal world. Humanity began to evolve as bloodlines altered due to interaction
with these otherworldly creatures.

Magic, lost to the ages, was revived, and governments formed secret agencies to
maintain the chaos at bay. Now in the current times, the supernatural thrives
but is controlled by corporations, governments, and ancient orders. One of these
entities are the Harbingers - a mysterious and profound organization which are
behind our protagonists, giving them power in exchange for their services.
