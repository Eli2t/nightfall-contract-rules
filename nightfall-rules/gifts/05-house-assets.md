# House Assets

## 4-Point Assets

### 4 - Filter Lungs Condition:

Condition: Either through smoking, hazardous health conditions, or natural
adaptation, your lungs filter out toxins and bad airs. (Any Body Roll made to
resist noxious fumes, hazardous gases, or foul odors, are made with +2 Dice.)

### 4 - Bootlicker.

Condition: You know what to say and how to act around the authorities. (Social
rolls made towards appealing to law enforcement, police or similar authorities,
are made with +2 Dice.)

### 4 - (INSERT) Rank

Circumstance: You have held, or currently hold a Rank in the [INSERT], of
which you are a [INSERT]. (You receive +1 Dice in Social Rolls where your
Rank matters, and depending on what your Rank is & what it pertains; you may be
granted certain benefits and/or opportunities at the GM's discretion. You are
guaranteed up to 1 "petition" of your Rank or colleagues a Contract /
Downtime--and particularly absurd or egregious petitions may be rejected or lead
to Loose Ends / temporary suspension)

- Typically represents field / service positions.

- Examples
  - Catholic Church, Priest
    - Pastoral care, administers sacraments, manages parish / church duties at
      the local level.

  - US Military, Sergeant
    - Supervises and leads a squad; trains and manages enlistees.

  - FBI (or DSPA) Special Agent
    - Operates in the field; investigations, intelligence, surveillance, etc.

  - Police, Detective.
    - Investigates crimes, gathers evidence, leads investigations.

  - Sorcerer Society, Grade 4 Sorcerer.
    - Field sorcerers; typically handles low-level threats (to homes or streets)
      and minor incidents.

    - Some belong to major Mahobatsus (Clans / Corporations), but the vast
      majority are idle freelancers.

  - Academia, Professor / “Doctor” / Post-Doctoral (PHD).

  - Alternatively: Research Scientist / Scientist.
    - Someone who has made it through the field, and either teaches, or is in
      the field.

    - Someone who designs, works on, and leads research projects. Has people
      beneath them (juniors and interns) but also above them.

  - Corporate, Manager.
    - Oversees a team or department, project management, etc.

### 4 - Acute Sense

Condition: A sense other than sight is heightened, and particularly acute. (Any
Perception rolls that rely on your select sense, are rolled at +2 Dice).

### 4 - Jumpman.

Condition: You may jump twice as far, twice as high, and you do not need to roll
in Combat to make a Jump.

- The Jump still takes up your Free Movement, however.

### 4 - Normal.

Condition: You are normal and completely generic appearance-wise. (Attempts to
recognize you, find you by face, or otherwise pick you out of a crowd, are at up
to +2 Difficulty.)

- This may not apply as usual if your "normal" is not normal, IE: you are a
  white man, and you go to Cambodia, or Turkmenistan.

### 4 - Minor

Condition: You are a Minor, and people tend to take you / that less seriously.
(Any social roll where being a Minor would help, are rolled at up to -2
Difficulty.)

### 4 - Elder

Condition: You are an elder or senior citizen; people either respect you or pity
you. (Any social roll where being an elder would help, are rolled at up to -2
Difficulty.)

- You've gotta be at least like 70 or something to be an Elder.

### 4 - Fashion Sense

Condition: You always seem to know what's the best look, or the best fit, for an
occasion. (As long as you have some knowledge of where you're going, and who's
going to be there, you may prepare an Outfit for yourself, or others. When you
do so, you (and anyone you do this to) receive -1 Difficulty in situations
where your Fashion Sense matters).

- You may still have to actually purchase clothes that are drippy, at GM
  discretion.

### 4 - Archer

Condition: You're a somewhat practiced bowman, despite your size, state, or
stature. (You no longer need 3 Brawn to use a Bow) (edited)

## 8-Point Assets

### 8 - Animal Friend

Condition: Animals love you, you find yourself at peace with Animals, and
Animals, at Peace with you. (You receive +2 Dice to any social Rolls with
Animals. If an Animal is aggressive, or attacks; you may Exert Mind to remove
yourself as a target to the Animal. Attacking or otherwise presenting yourself
as a valid target negates this Asset.)

### 8 - Absolute Direction

Condition: You always know where you are going, and how to get back on track.
(You have +2 Dice to navigate unfamiliar terrain, you may Exert Mind whenever
you find yourself lost & stuck, to reorient & put yourself back on track.)

### 8 - Sharpshooter

Condition: Your aim is sharp, and your shots are too. (Aiming with a Firearm now
only requires a Quick Action--you may make any reaction against the target you
are Concentrating on aiming against.)

### 8 - Onomatopoeian

Condition: Someway, somehow, you can replicate, to similar volume, any sound you
hear or have heard. (You may, by ear, replicate any sound you have recently
heard, or hear, including gunshots, fireworks, animal sounds, radio sounds, gun
clicks, or similar.)

### 8 - Well Traveled

Condition: You've either been to alot of places, talked to alot of people, or
find picking up phrases fun; either way, you conveniently remember / have useful
phrases come to mind. (You may Exert Mind to "remember" or conveniently
understand up to 3 seconds (about a sentence) of any non-Alien language; when
you do so, you may respond as well. You may do this so long as you remember the
sentence.)

### 8 - Recoil-Ready

Condition: You've trained to handle recoil, and you know how to swing things...
despite your stature. (For the purpose of Handling / Recoil rules, you are
treated as having a level higher of Brawn.)

- Requires less than 3 Brawn.

### 8 - Survivor.

Condition: You regularly go long without eating, drinking, and find it easy and
normal to starve yourself. (You receive +2 Dice to any Body Rolls made against
Exposure / against the elements, hunger, thirst, etc).

### 8 - Acute Sight

Condition: Your sense of sight is sharp, heightened, and potentially more
precise. (Any Perception roll you make that majorly involves Sight, is at -1
Difficulty.)

### 8 - Speedloader

Condition: You've a knack for quick-loading, reload tricks, and mag-swaps.
(Reloading a Firearm is now a Quick Action for you, if reloading a Firearm (or
similar) which would cost more than one Action–it costs one less.)

### 8 - Inhuman

Condition: You aren't exactly Human; and this helps you in some way. (You have
+1 Dice to [Action or task your Paradigm helps in], and for the purpose of
Effects or targeting, you no longer count as Human.]

### 8 - Immortal

Condition: The passage of time takes no toll on you. (You do not age & any
supernatural attempt to age you is at +2 Difficulty (if not, you may Exert to
"auto succeed" against the Effect)

- Requires/replaces 9 - Ageless (17 total EXP)

### 8 - Steeled

Condition: You can function under extreme amounts of pain. (Stress from Injuries
is reduced by 3.)

- Requires 6 - Tough (Total: 14 EXP)

### 8 - Locked In

Condition: You remain locked-in during even the worst crises. (Stress from Mind
Damage is reduced by 3). Replaces focused.

- Requires 6 - Focused (Total: 14 EXP)

### 8 - Water-Breathing

Condition: You find that you can breathe water just as you can breathe air. (You
can breathe in water as if you were breathing in air.)

- No, this does not affect low-oxygen in places like high mountains, or
  space--or similarly vacuums.

### 8 - McGuffin

Condition: You pull out the most cinematically convenient items at the most
perfect times. (You may Exert to pull out / "conveniently remember" or otherwise
find an item not listed on your Stock or that is convenient for the situation.
Such items are limited to what would fit in your bag or container and/or on your
person. At the GM's discretion, such as after being searched, certain items may
not be available.)

- Examples.
  - Pulling out a razor blade from a fake tooth.

  - Reaching into your pockets and conveniently discovering in the seam of your
    pockets a lockpick.

### 14 - High Ranking (INSERT)

Circumstance: You are a high-ranking [INSERT] of the [INSERT]. Your position
allows you to get things, and get away with things that most otherwise wouldn't.
(You receive up to +2 Dice to any Social Rolls where your Rank matters; and

- Typically represents middle management.

- Examples:
  - Catholic Church, Monsignor

  - US Military, Major
    - Commands a battalion, manages operations and staff; a senior officer in
      the field.

  - FBI (or DSPA), Supervisory (/ Senior) Special Agent
    Or; some sort of “Head” at a facility or supersite. (Head of Security,
    Research, etc).
    - Oversees a team of Special Agents, manages cases and coordinates
      investigations across multiple units.

  - Police, Lieutenant
    - Manages multiple detectives & officers and handles administrative duties
      in a precinct.

  - Sorcerer Society, Grade 3 Sorcerer.
    - Handles threats to city blocks or small towns; handles higher-profile
      incidents.

    - Minimum Grade required to work as a teacher at a sorcery school, or
      similar.

    - Most typically mentor, instruct, lead, or teach, lower grade sorcerers.

  - Academia, Tenured Professor / Principal Investigator
    - Experienced academic with tenure; responsible for advanced research,
      higher-level teaching, and department leadership.

    - Leads major research projects, responsible for overall project management,
      securing grants, and directing research activities. Holds significant
      authority in research settings.

  - Corporate, Director.
    - Oversees one or more departments / business units; reports to senior
      executives.
