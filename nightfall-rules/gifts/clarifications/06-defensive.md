## Obscure

### Invisibility: Being "Discovered"

If you are "discovered" while invisible; the target will be able to reliably
track you as if you were visible, for either a minute OR until you manage to
majorly restealth / "get away" from their Line of Sight.

- You leave footprints in most terrain still.

- Rain will stop around you / water, snow, fog, mist, flour in the air--all will
  "slick" / "cover" you.

- You block light; someone shining a flashlight on you would obviously
  "discover" you as they would see, then, your shadow.

- Other people's blood / flour in the air / sand / dirt / bullets / etc, will
  all still be visible when they stick / land / hit you.

- Parts of you that leave you, such as your own blood, or your limbs, remain
  Invisible so long as you do.

- Restealthing / re-switching your Invisibility, does not impart Invisibility
  onto things like bullets, arrows, swords impaled into you, or similar.

### Environmental Factors

A list of a few environmental factors that interfere with your Invisibility.

- The Effect grants no Difficulty increases or Dice reductions against them on
  its own, for this duration.

### Visibility Requirements

Any Gifts or Effects you use or activate still, have their normal visibility
requirements--including Disturbings.

Being Invisible does not suddenly negate the visibility drawback on Gifts /
Effects--they must still be visible, and/or Disturbing, if possible, even if you
are Invisible.

## Phase Out

### General

Q: When do I phase back in?
A: On your initiative [Time Period] after you Phased out.
(If you had a Round of Phase-Out; you'd phase back in on your turn, the next
Round)

### Intangibility

If you "phase back" into an object--you take an INJURY (regardless of Armor)
equal to the following--as whatever phased back in is deleted from existence.

- If your hand phased back into something.
  - Severity 5 + Major Scar ; Missing Hand.

- If your Arm or Leg phased back into something else.
  - Severity 6 + Severe Scar ; Missing Arm (or Leg)

- If multiple Limbs phased into something else.
  - Two Severity 6s + Severe Scar Missing Limb(s)

- If your Head, Heart, or similar, phased back into something else.
  - Severity 9 / Instant Death.

- If your entire body phases back into something else.
  - You are instantly dead, and likely erased from existence.
