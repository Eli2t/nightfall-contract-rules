## Environmental Control

### The Veil Buff

The Zone: Veil now, also, prevents any recording devices inside the Zone from
capturing any Alien phenomena; any Alien phenomena captured from inside the zone
is instead captured as its mundane equivalent.

### No Comms Buff

Zone Effect: No Comms, also, means that you cannot send text messages, signals,
wireless transmissions, impulses–no attempt to communicate with things outside a
No Comms Zone should ever be successful.

## Summon Minions

### Armor Clarification

Minions in Nightfall are no longer allowed to have armor unless it is from a
passive or active power from the familiar effect.

### Armored/Golem Nerf

Armored (and golem) can no longer be taken with squad.
