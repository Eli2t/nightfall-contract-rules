## Detect X

### Detect Beings Criteria

Must apply to less than half of all Animate targets. E.g. "humans", "criminals",
"robots", "the undead"

Acceptable Beings:

- Maneaters
  - A being that regularly and purposefully consumes people.

- Extraterrestrials
  - A supernatural entity that originates from outside Earth; aliens from outer
    space.

- Zoanthropes
  - People who alternate between animal & person due to possessing Viral
    Zoanthropy or similar supernatural conditions.

- Monsters
  - Monstrous Supernatural Cryptids, Creatures & similar.

- Sorcerers
  - Anyone who can cast or channel innate energy / Source into magical,
    mystical, or spiritual Effects.

- Spooks
  - Anyone who works for a government Intelligence / Investigations Agency.

- Fae
  - Supernatural beings with magical abilities and a close connection to
    nature. They can vary in size from tiny to human-sized or larger, and are
    often of European origin.

- Demons
  - An inhuman entity, often malevolent or evil, which almost always incarnates
    specifically to cause harm, chaos, temptation, or otherwise curse people and
    people kind. These beings are almost always Unliving, but not Undead.
  - This covers certain Yokai concepts, such as the Oni or Tengu, as well as
    Abrahamic ideas of Demons.
  - This does NOT cover vengeful spirits such as the Yurei, as those would be
    Undead & thus fall under Undead.

- Undead
  - A being that while technically dead, is still Animate.
  - Such as zombies, vampires, spirits, etc.

## Investigate X

### Info Enhancement Cap

The amount of information-revealing enhancements you can attach to Investigate X
(Individual, Area, Object) is limited by win bracket:

- 2 at Newbie (0-3 Wins)
- 3 at Newbie-Novice (3-6 Wins)
- 4 at High Novice (6-9 Wins)
- 5 at Low Seasoned (9-12 Wins)
- 6 at Seasoned (12-15 Wins)
- 7 at Professional (15-18/19)
- 8 at the Vet-Gate (20 & 20+)
- 9 at Veteran (25 & 25+)
