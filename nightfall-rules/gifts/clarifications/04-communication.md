## Send Message

### Requirements / Constituents

All Send Messages must abide by the following rules.

- The Target MUST be made intuitively aware they are receiving a message.
  Targets are free to ignore the message as they choose.
  - They are not typically made aware of the sender, or anything beyond what's
    contained in the message.

If it has Flat Rate Box:

- The target is also made intuitively aware they are receiving a package, but
  are not made aware what it is; they may choose to accept, or deny, the
  package.
  - The sender still pays the Exertion cost as normal.

### Flying Paper & Lag Combo

Flying Paper's "your message may be intercepted in transit" is meant to combo
with Laaag; if taken with Laag, the message takes 30 minutes to arrive and is
intuitively interceptable.

Examples:

- If it is literally a flying paper; it takes 30 minutes to arrive at the
  target, and can be intercepted / picked up on the way there.

- If it's a snake who whispers your message to people; it takes 30 minutes to
  get to them, and can be intercepted / picked up on the way there.

### Flat-Rate Box

The following applies to certain objects sent w/ Flat Rate Box.

- Any Wards placed on an object sent through w/ Flat Rate Box are immediately
  "popped"--as are any Marks, or similarly Illusions.

- Any GPS / tracking devices sent with Flat Rate Box are also "popped"--they
  stop working or cease function the moment they are sent through w/ Flat Rate
  Box.

- You cannot use Flat Rate Box to send "traps" of any kind, you can't use it to
  GPS-Tap people, you can't use it to send bombs in the mail, it is not an
  offensive Gift.
