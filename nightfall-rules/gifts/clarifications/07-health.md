## From Death Life

Does not retain Live for Rot's "Revived creatures that have died can only be
raised Once" stipend.

### "Full Health" Meaning

Contractors are raised with full Body Level; but retain any Battle Scars /
Physical Conditions they had in life.

## Raise Dead

### Raise Dead + Contractors

In order to effectively raise a Contractor as a Contractor; your Raise Dead
requires The Spark & Lich.

Contractors resurrected through Raise Dead may continue The Contract / the
Cosmic Conspiracy as usual. (Under playtesting.)

### The Hunger Clarification

The following are legitimate for Raise Dead's The Hunger.

(Note; it must be logistically challenging, hard to reliably obtain,
particularly problematic or some combination of the two.)

- Your fill of another person's flesh.
  - What you would normally consume as a person; except it must be another
    person's flesh.

- A Soul of the dead or dying.
  - Perform a short obviously Disturbing ritual with a target that has recently
    died (at most an hour).

- A gallon of someone else's blood.
  - A lethal amount.

- A significant source of power or energy, (100 KW)
  - About an hour of "charging" at a city service transformer, or electric feed
    line.
