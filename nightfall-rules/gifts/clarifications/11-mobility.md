## Repel (Push / Pull)

### Requires a Roll

Repel requires a roll to activate & to resist.

Choose Attribute + Ability @ 6.
Choose Dodge or Defend, Mind @ 7, or Body @ 7.

### Speed Bonus + F1/Zoom

The "Speed Bonus" / Parameter in Nightfall on Superspeed multiplies both the
additional feet/round you can add to your sprint or accelerated speed & also the
maximum amount of additional feet/round you can "accelerate" that up to.

Examples

- If a target sprints 100 feet in a Round, and has "Quadrupled" Speed Parameter
  & F1…
  - They may add up to 1200 feet/Round to this, up to a maximum of 3600
    feet/Round.
    - This would put them initially at F1 Speed, then Plane Speed, and finally
      Jet Speed.

Note

- Without ancillary Gifts; controlling one's self or remaining safe during
  incredibly high speeds is extraordinarily & exponentially difficult.
