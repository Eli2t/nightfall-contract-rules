## Affecting Intangibles

The following, explicitly, affects targets even if they are intangible from an
Effect. This assumes the target has a physical body.

- Any Effect with Conduit, or Voodoo Doll, affects the Intangible target.
  - They still are required to be usable on Animate targets, if applicable.

- Any Effect that only requires interaction with the target, or perceiving the
  target; such as Investigative Effects, the majority of Influence Effects, or
  similar.
  - They still are required to be usable on Animate targets, if applicable.

- The Traumatize Effect.

- Restrict with Ethereal Chains.
