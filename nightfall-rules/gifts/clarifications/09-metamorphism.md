## Alternate Form

### Uniform

You must actually be able to obtain / restock your Uniform; any items that you
are not currently holding / are not in your possession when you "uniform" leave
your Uniform.

Sapient Objects may Easily Resist being put in your uniform.

### Different Body & Death

Battlescars are tracked separately; your Different Body must be tracked in your
Stock and recovers at the same rate that you do.

If it dies; your Different Body is unavailable for a single Contract &
Downtime--this is represented by a Loose End on your sheet that indicates such.

GMs: make sure you assign it after you conclude a Contract, if it dies on the
Contract.

## Creature Transformation

You may choose "A [SIZED] version of me" as a Cryptid choice.

You use the Contract's stock stats for Creatures it has on the Gift; not the
stat blocks we have here.

## Inhuman Physiology

### Tooth N' Claw

Tooth N' Claw Weapons benefit from the tertiary features of any Weapons they
emulate, but may only emulate "standard" weapons. (Not explosive ones.)

All Tooth N'Claw Weapons take a Quick Action to equip / unequip; and it is
obviously a Weapon when equipped.

At the GM's discretion; Battle Scars you receive may temporarily disable or
damage Tooth N' Claw Weapons; it requires at minimum a Major Battlescar to do
so.

Armor Penetration is limited to 2, if such is a tertiary feature.

## Warp Form

Warp Form is defended at @6 difficulty. Additionally, each scar requires an
additional contested outcome.

- 5 For Extreme
- 4 For Severe
- 3 For Major
- 1-2 Minor
