## Hail Storm
*Enhancement on Archery Mastery and Thrown Weapon*

Instead of exerting your mind to attack all targets in a 120 degree arc, Hail
Storm now simply allows bows to make a full-auto sweep against targets in a 120
degree arc. (Attack @ 8, no longer requires an exertion)

## Mythic Attributes
*Mythic Brawn, Mythic Charisma, Mythic Dexterity, Mythic Intellect, Mythic Perception*

### Exert For Guaranteed Success

Exerting for a guaranteed 5 success can only be used once per contract.

### Samson Nerf

The Samson drawback (available on all Mythic Attribute gifts) has the following
change: When using Will to Survive on an attack, roll high or low. If the high
or low fails, your Samson gets destroyed and you lose all benefits for the next
2 months. This may either be a replacement to the Battle Scar you would've
taken, or in addition, at GM discretion and depending on severity.
