# Stock Battlescars

The online Character Sheets provide a helpful selector for pre-made Battle
Scars, complete with systems; however, GMs may design custom Battle Scars as
well. The following are some sample Battlescars exclusive to Nightfall.

Note: It’s up to GMs to determine which Battle Scars are / aren’t applicable to
be “given out”.

## Minor Scars (Sev 4)

Near-Sighted

- You suffer from nearsightedness. (All Perception rolls requiring far-sight, or
  sight that is any further than five feet from you, are rolled at +2
  Difficulty.)

Damaged [ ]

- Your [ ] is damaged. (You suffer +1 Difficulty to all Actions utilizing [
  ] )
  - Choose Hands, Feet, Arms, Legs, Ears, Eyes, etc.

Concussive Trauma

- Any action which requires Concentration or focus are at +1 Difficulty & 1
  Dice Penalty.

Weak Knees

- Your knees are damaged and/or you lack support: you may carry 5 less Pounds
  per point of Brawn, and you must roll in order to stand up without aid.

Skin Sensitivity

- You are at 1 Dice Penalty when exposed to Sunlight.

Unusual Presentation

- You have some sort of unusual & highly recognizable; be it incredibly
  excessive piercings or high-profile cosmetics, highly visible full-body
  tattoos, or other very alienating features that make you very easily
  recognizable.

## Major Scars (Sev 5)

Arthritis

- Your joints are inflamed, swelled, and tender. (You suffer -2 Dice to all
  Physical Actions / Reactions.)

Torn ACL

- You are no longer able to perform an All-Out Sprint, or Dash.

Brain Damage

- You must Exert Mind to Concentrate; and any Intellect roll you make (or
  majorly cognitive rolls) is at +2 Difficulty.

Albinism

- You are at 2 Dice Penalty in Sunlight, and you appear exceptionally
  pale-skinned with red eyes.

Missing Lung

- You are at +2 Difficulty to All-Out Sprint, and without air, you suffocate in
  Rounds instead of Minutes.

Social Pariah

- You are obviously unusual &/or supernatural: this causes complications for you
  wherever you go, and you are at up to +2 Difficulty in social situations
  where this is relevant. This may cause social complications.

- Examples
  - Being covered head to toe in religious and/or magical tattoos–these must
    obviously be, to anyone who sees them, of esoteric nature. These cover your
    entire body.

  - Being an anthropomorphic "animal person" qualifies for this.

  - Being obviously a walking dead body qualifies.

  - Being an obvious cyborg and/or robot qualifies.

Haywire

- Your mind is improperly wired & you suffer regularly from it. (You are at a
  minimum of 1 mental stress at all times.)

Chronic Pain

- You are under a constant state of pain. (You are at a minimum of 1 physical
  stress at all times.)

Feeble Constitution

- Your immune system and/or vitality is especially feeble. (You have +2
  Difficulty on any and all Body rolls you make.)

Weary & Withered

- You're not exactly in the prime of your life anymore. (Your Body Rating is 1
  level lower than it should be; represented on sheet by a permanent &
  unhealable "Injury")

Minor Narcoleptic

- You have a tendency to pass out at especially inconvenient / important times.
  (When combat, or otherwise intense time-sensitive situations begin, roll Body.
  In Outcome in Rounds; you will fall asleep, and will remain asleep until a
  major disrupting event (such as a severity 1 Injury or resistance roll) wakes
  you--but if you roll a full success or higher (4/5); you do not fall asleep at
  all.)

No Flow State

- You don't quite enter your "Flow-State" the same way that most seem to
  do--your body doesn't quite kick into gear. (Exertion no longer provides
  +Outcome & instead of ignoring Stress to your entire Round, only applies to
  the roll you use it on)

De-finged

- You are missing key fingers. (You are missing key-fingers; you have -2 Dice
  to any roll which would require the use of your hands / fingers.)

Fragile

- You are fragile; any Armor you might have, intrinsic or otherwise, is worth 1
  points less.

## Severe Scars (Sev 6)

Severe Brain Trauma

- Whenever you would Exert to gain Outcome, or ignore Stress, roll a High or
  Low. On a failure; you do not gain Outcome or Ignore Stress.

Disturbingly Disfigured

- You are undeniably & undoubtedly Disturbing & Disfigured-at-best: you have +3
  Difficulty to any and all social rolls involving your appearance, and your
  mere presence, may cause complications and/or discrimination.

Embrittled

- You have become especially brittle; any Armor you might have, intrinsic or
  otherwise, is worth 2 points less.

Damaged Frontal Lobe

- Your frontal lobe has been damaged: you have -3 Dice to any and all rolls
  made in social situations, including ones made for empathetic or
  emotional-gauging purposes. Whenever you fail a Trauma roll, you always
  receive a Trauma as well as Mind Damage.

Nervous Damage

- Any physical Actions / Reactions you make suffer from a -2 Dice Penalty, this
  stacks with any Stress you may have.

Epilepsy

- You are prone to seizures that may occur unpredictably. You have a tendency to
  pass out at especially inconvenient / important times. (When combat, or
  otherwise intense time-sensitive situations begin, roll Body. In Outcome in
  Rounds; you will begin seizing and will lose the ability to take/make any
  meaningful action or communication You will stop seizing after a minute has
  passed, or you receive "rescue medications"--but if you roll a full success or
  higher (4/5); you do not seize at all.)

## Extreme Scars (Sev 7 & 7+)

Extreme Brain Trauma

- Exerting Mind no longer nets you +Outcome or the ability to ignore Stress.

Extreme Nervous Damage

- Any physical Actions / Reactions you make suffer from a -3 Dice Penalty, this
  stacks with any Stress you may have.

Monstrous

- You are obviously a Disturbing Monster, you automatically botch the vast
  majority of social rolls pertaining to appearance.

Diminutive Respiration.

- You require a breathing apparatus of some sort to breathe; without it, you
  begin to suffocate in Rounds as if you were drowning or otherwise choking.

Barely Alive

- Your Body works overtime to merely sustain your life: Any Body Rolls you make
  are at +2 Difficulty, and all Unstabilized Injuries begin Degradation by the
  Round, and by the Minute if Concentration is done to keep them stable.

Halved

- You have been bisected, or rendered waist-down unable, it is a miracle you are
  even “alive”: Your entire lower half of your body is missing, likely including
  your legs & feet. You cannot use your legs or feet, as you likely do not have
  them anymore.
