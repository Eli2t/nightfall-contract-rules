# Animals

Stat blocks for terrestrial and aquatic predators.

## Terrestrial Predators

These stat blocks represent land predators that Contractors are likely to face
in combat

### Wolf

Wolves typically weigh in at about 80 - 100 LBs, are and operate in Packs of up
to 30 whose smallest units conveniently make up about 6 members–perfect to run
as Mobs of 5, with one scout / relay wolf.

**Wolf**
**General**
**Body**: 6 (2 Brawn)
**Mind**: 3 (1 Int, 1 Cha)

- If dropped to 0 Mind; continues to function–likely at a reduced, or enraged,
  state.

**Armor**: 1 (Intrinsic)
**Free Movement**: 50ft (4 Dex)

- If performing an All-Out Sprint, Free Movement + Dash * 2.

**Dash**: 10ft

**Combat**
**General** Dice: 8
**Combat** Dice: 7
**Attacks**:

- Maws n’ Claws (1 Weapon Damage)
  - Pounce: Grapples may deal damage equal to Contested Outcome w/out bonus
    Weapon Damage.
- Lunge
  - May lunge as an Action, or as apart of their Free Movement if closing the
    gap for an attack, up to 150 feet. They may only do so once per Hour.

**Special**

- **Sense of Smell**
  - May combat Invisible targets through their sense of smell; locating &
    attacking an invisible target is done at -2 Dice.
- **Speed**
  - Can sustain a Speed Rating of 1 over short-medium distances & durations (Up
    to 30-40 MPH, roughly, for 1-2 hours).

## Aquatic Predators

These stat blocks represent aquatic predators that Contractors are likely to
face in combat

### Small Shark (Whitetips & Sandbars

These Sharks are about the size of your average person–and weigh about as much
too.

**Small Shark**
**General**
**Body**: 7 (4 Brawn)
**Mind**: 3 (1 Int, 1 Cha)

- If dropped to 0 Mind; continues to function–likely at a reduced, or enraged,
  state.

**Armor**: 1 (Intrinsic)

- Deals back as much damage as their Armor prevents against unarmed attacks.

**Free Movement**: 50ft (4 Dex)

- If performing an All-Out Sprint, Free Movement + Dash * 2.

**Dash**: 20ft

**Combat**
**General** Dice: 8
**Combat** Dice: 9
**Attacks**:

- Maws n’ Claws (2 Weapon Damage)
  - Pounce: Grapples may deal damage equal to Contested Outcome w/out bonus
    Weapon Damage.
- Lunge
  - May lunge as an Action, or as apart of their Free Movement if closing the
    gap for an attack, up to 150 feet. They may only do so once per Hour.

**Special**

- **Shark Senses**
  - May combat Invisible targets through their sense of smell, as well as other
    senses; locating & attacking an invisible target is done at -2 Dice.
  - If a target is within 300 feet; the roll to detect / locate them is made at
    -2 Difficulty.
  - If a target is bleeding (IE: Unstabilized Injury) ; the roll automatically
    succeeds.
