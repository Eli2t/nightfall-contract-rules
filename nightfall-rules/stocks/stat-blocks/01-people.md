# Stat Blocks

## People

Covers mundane people; baseline Humans that possess no ability to channel Source
(or Source at all, really), and wield typically mundane & ordinary weaponry and
skills found / possessed by those in the real world.

### Civilians

These stat blocks represent regular people from varying occupations / walks of
everyday life.

#### An Average Person

Represents the absolute average person in almost each and every respect.
 IE: A highschooler walking home.

- Unarmed Attack (-1 WDMG)

## Criminals

The statblocks below represent violent criminals whose lifestyles heavily and
directly revolve around combat and/or the threat of it.

Represents not hardened gangbangers, but just about anyone from any walk of life
who might / would decide to start trouble.
 IE: People who go out seeking bar fights.
 IE: Unsavory men who chase girls at night.

combat encounters.

    lose their next Action. Otherwise, If they botch, they are stunned and also

    early if the darts are removed or if they roll an Outcome of at least 4

  - Targets directly hit take 10 damage. Targets within 5 feet take 15 dice of
    Damage +2, decaying by 1 Damage per 5 feet out, stopping entirely at 50
    feet
- Flashbang (100 / 200 / 300)
  - Radius 20 feet. Make a Body resistance roll at Difficulty 8.
  - Everyone within that range suffers Physical Stress increased by 7 -
    Outcome, decaying one level per Round.
  - Affected targets are deafened for 30 seconds. If inside or in a dark area,
    affected targets are blinded for one Round
