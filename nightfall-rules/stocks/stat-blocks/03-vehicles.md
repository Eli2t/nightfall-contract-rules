# Vehicles

Covers stat blocks for vehicles as combat obstacles

## Military Land Vehicles

These stat blocks represent military land vehicles as combat obstacles, such as
armored cars, guntrucks, tanks, etc.

### Guntruck

A Guntruck is a vehicle that has been outfitted with armor and/or a gun.
Typically Gun Trucks are civilian trucks, such as Toyota Technicals, jury rigged
w/ machine guns by insurgents or paramilitaries.

**Guntruck**
**General**
**Body**: 14

- Cumulative Body; tracked Linearly.
- If reduced to 0; the Vehicle is destroyed.

**Armor:** 6 (or 3, if lacking Armor Plating)

- Called Shots against critical components.
  - Tires have 1 Armor and 2 “Body”.
    - For every tire lost; +1 Difficulty and -1 Max Speed Rating.
  - Engine has 6 Armor and 4 “Body”.
    - Any damage to the Engine drops the maximum Speed Rating by 1 per point of
      Damage, if Destroyed; the Vehicle is immobile.

**Free Movement**: 50ft

**Crew**
**Crew Actions:** The Crew uses Mob Rules, but the Driver may choose to make a
Reaction / Action to make Maneuvers; such as dodging for the Crew.

- Gunner
  - The Gunner may use their Action to make an Attack
- Driver
  - The Driver may use their Action or Reaction to make a Dodge roll for the
    Vehicle.
- Passengers
  - If possible; Passengers may use their Actions to make attacks from within
    the Vehicle.
    - Note: if they do so on the same Round that the Gunner’s Heavy Machine Gun
      fires–use Mob Outcome.

**Attacking the Crew:** Attacking a specific Crew Member requires a Called Shot
against a Small Target, and without an opening, the Crew Member benefits from
the Vehicle’s Armor, as well as from any attempt by the Driver to Dodge or
otherwise make Maneuvers.

- If an opening is present, a Called Shot against a Tiny Target is required to
  make an attack against the specific Passenger / Crew member–without the
  Passenger benefiting from the Vehicle’s Armor.

**Combat**
**General** Dice: 7

- The Driver rolls this, for any Maneuvers they make.

**Combat** Dice: 8
**Attacks**:

- Heavy Machine Gun (5 WDMG) (2000/4000/6000) (10RR)
  - Full Auto Sweeps are rolled at +1 Difficulty, and only consume 3 Rounds of
    Ammo.
  - Penetrates 3 Armor.
- Assault Rifle (3 WDMG) (300/600/900) (5RR)

**Special**

- **Acceleration:** The Driver may, with their Free Movement, choose to
  Accelerate up, or down, a single Speed Rating. This Vehicle’s Max Speed Rating
  is 3.
  - Any Attacks made while the Vehicle is traveling any faster than its Free
    Movement are made at +2 Difficulty.

### APC

APCs, or Armored Personnel Carriers, are heavily armored vehicles whose main
purpose is to transport troops into battle. Many APCs also are equipped with
weapons systems; such as Heavy Machine Guns, or even tank turrets.

**General**
**Body**: 20

- Cumulative Body; tracked Linearly.
- If reduced to 0; the Vehicle is destroyed.

**Armor:** 10

- Called Shots against critical components.

    against a tiny target.

      passengers.
  - Tires have 6 Armor and 4 “Body”.
    - For every tire lost; +1 Difficulty and -1 Max Speed Rating.

- Driver
  - The Driver may use their Action or Reaction to make a Dodge roll for the
    Vehicle.

## Military Aircraft

These stat blocks represent Military Aircraft; such as attack helicopters,
fighter jets, etc.

### Light Attack Helicopter.

Light Attack Helicopters are light attack helicopters; small micro-crewed
aircraft with minimal Armor, small crews, and small payloads. They typically do
“Drive Bys”; engaging targets from about 1000 ft up in the air, at 100MPH.
Famous Example: The Littlebird.

**Light Attack Helicopter**
**General**
**Body**: 14

- Cumulative Body; tracked Linearly.
- If reduced to 0; the Vehicle is destroyed.

**Armor:** 6

- Called Shots against critical components.
  - The Main Rotor has 6 Armor and 7 “Body”.
    - Generally requires a Called Shot against a Small Target.
    - Destroying the Main Rotor results in the Driver losing the ability to
      control ascent or descent; generally meaning a Catastrophic Crash.
  - The Tail Rotor has 6 Armor and 4 “Body”
    - Generally requires a Called Shot against a Tiny Target.
    - Destroying the Tail Rotor results in the Driver losing the ability to
      steer / control the Vehicle; generally resulting in a Catastrophic Crash.
  - The Engine is generally inside the Vehicle; and thus benefits fully from the
    Vehicle’s Armor.
    - The Engine itself has 6 Armor and 4 “Body”.
    - Any damage to the Engine drops the maximum Speed Rating by 1 per point of
      Damage, if Destroyed; the Vehicle is immobile.
    - Requires a Called Shot against a Small Target.
  - Windshields provide 4 Armor to Passengers / Crew targeted by Called Shots.
    - Generally requires a Called Shot against a Small Target.

**Free Movement**: 200ft

**Crew**
**Crew Actions:** The Crew uses Mob Rules, but the Driver may choose to make a
Reaction / Action to make Maneuvers; such as dodging for the Crew.

- Gunner
  - The Gunner may use their Action to make an Attack.
- Pilot
  - The Driver may use their Action or Reaction to make a Dodge roll for the
    Vehicle.
- Passengers

    the Vehicle.

      fires–use Mob Outcome.

otherwise make Maneuvers.

- If an opening is present, a Called Shot against a Tiny Target is required to
  make an attack against the specific Passenger / Crew member–without the
  Passenger benefiting from the Vehicle’s Armor.

**Combat**
**General** Dice: 9

- The Pilot rolls this for any and all maneuvers; at -1 Difficulty.

**Combat** Dice: 9

- The Gunner rolls this for any and all attacks; at -1 Difficulty.

**Attacks**:

- Rotary Cannon (6 WDMG) (2000/4000/6000) (10RR)
  - Full Auto Sweeps are rolled at +1 Difficulty.
  - Every attack is a full auto sweep that covers a 50x50 area.
  - Penetrates 4 Armor.

  - Targets within 5 feet take 15@6 & +3 Damage, decaying by 1 Damage per 5ft

  - Typically only carries up to 4.
    - If it fires multiple at once; +1 Outcome for each missile it fires. (Up

**Special**

  Accelerate up, or down, a single Speed Rating. This Vehicle’s Max Speed Rating

  - Any Attacks made while the Vehicle is traveling any faster than its Free
    Movement are made at +2 Difficulty.

  which confuse oncoming heat seeking / missiles & “take them” for the Vehicle.

**Armor:** 8

- Called Shots against critical components.
  - The Engine is generally inside the Vehicle; and thus benefits fully from the
    Vehicle’s Armor.
    - The Engine itself has 6 Armor and 4 “Body”.
    - Any damage to the Engine drops the maximum Speed Rating by 1 per point of
      Damage, if Destroyed; the Vehicle is immobile.
    - Requires a Called Shot against a Small Target.
  - Windshields provide 6 Armor to Passengers / Crew targeted by Called Shots.
    - Generally requires a Called Shot against a Small Target.

**Free Movement**: 3000ft

**Crew**
**Crew Actions:** The Crew for this Vehicle is typically a singular person.

- Ace Pilot
  - The Pilot may, as a part of Free Movement, make a Maneuver (such as
    Dodging).
  - The Pilot may use their Action or Reaction to use any of the Vehicle’s
    functions as normal.

**Attacking the Crew:** Attacking a specific Crew Member requires a Called Shot
against a Small Target, and without an opening, the Crew Member benefits from
the Vehicle’s Armor, as well as from any attempt by the Driver to Dodge or
otherwise make Maneuvers.

- If an opening is present, a Called Shot against a Tiny Target is required to
  make an attack against the specific Passenger / Crew member–without the
  Passenger benefiting from the Vehicle’s Armor.

**Combat**
**General** Dice: 14

- The Pilot rolls this for any and all maneuvers; at -1 Difficulty.

**Combat** Dice: 14

- The Pilot rolls this for any and all attacks; at -1 Difficulty.

**Attacks**:

- Aircraft Cannon (6 WDMG) (7500/15000/22500) (2RR)
  - Full Auto Sweeps have no increased Difficulty.
  - Every attack is a full auto sweep that covers a 50x50 area.
  - Penetrates 4 Armor.
- AGM Missile (7500 / 15000 / 22500)
  - When fired; hits 25ft - (5ft & Contested Outcome) away from the intended
    target–if a direct hit; the target takes 15 damage flat w/ 5 points of armor
    penetration.
  - Targets within 5 feet take 15@6 & +3 Damage, decaying by 1 Damage per 5ft
    away–stopping completely at 50ft.
    - Typically only carries up to 8.
      - If it fires multiple at once; +1 Outcome for each missile it fires. (Up
        to a maximum of +5.)
- Assault Rifle (3 WDMG) (300/600/900) (5RR)

**Special**

- **Acceleration:** The Driver may, with their Free Movement, choose to
  Accelerate up, or down, 2 Speed Ratings. This Vehicle’s Max Speed Rating is
  7.
  - Any Attacks made while the Vehicle is traveling any faster than its Free
    Movement are made at +2 Difficulty.
  - Any Attacks made against the Vehicle while it is traveling faster than its
    Free Movement are made at +2 Difficulty.
- **Flares!:** The Driver may use their Reaction (or Action) to deploy Flares;
  which confuse oncoming heat seeking / missiles & “take them” for the Vehicle.
