# Fascinate

*Get someone's attention*

**Category:** Influence

**Vectors:** Targeted, Trap

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Consumable Crafting, Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You have a method of catching and holding the attention of other creatures. This effect is transmitted by a particular sense (sight, hearing, smell, etc), and while you can generally stop someone in their tracks, an active life-or-death struggle will always take priority.

## Enhancements

- **Ammo**
  This consumable is meant to be used as ammo in a ranged weapon, applied to a melee weapon as poison, or to be thrown. It uses the standard attack roll and range for the selected attack type. The attack itself does no damage, but the Effect may.
  *Errata: Attack types include: firearms, thrown, bow-and-arrow, sling, etc. The Attack roll used to activate this Consumable cannot be at a Difficulty lower than 6. You may only use one Consumable per Action.*
  Detail required: Attack type

- **Battery-operated** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Instead of Exerting Mind to activate this Effect, it has its own 3-point Source pool. The Source regenerates at the rate of one point per day and cannot be raised with Experience.
  *Errata: Different Effects on the same Artifact with this Enhancement share a battery pool. Each additional Battery-Operated after the first increases the capacity of the Source by 3, to a maximum of 12.*

- **Battle Cry**
  The Effect no longer ends if violence occurs nearby or if your target is Injured. It does end if the target takes Damage from a source other than you.
  *Errata: Affected targets may still Attack you.*

- **Cantrip** [Targeted]
  Activating this Effect has no Exertion cost, but it can have no more than 3 Drawbacks and its Gift Cost can never be increased above 2.

- **Center Of Attention**
  You may select a number of targets up to your Charisma rating. Audiences or mobs with a singular focus count as one target.

- **Conditionally Costless** [Targeted | Seasoned]
  This Effect does not require Exertion in certain circumstances. The condition should be balanced such that it has an activation cost the majority of the time you are able to use it.
  *Errata: If you take this Enhancement, you must be able to use the Effect at full cost and other times at no cost. 

This cannot be the same condition as the Drawback "Conditional" as that would imply that the Effect is costless any time you can cast it.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Dazzle**
  You may activate this Effect as a Reaction to contest any Action a valid target is taking. The target does not roll to resist, and beating their Outcome does not fascinate them. This interrupts Concentration.

- **Durable** [Consumable Crafting]
  When you use this Consumable, roll a single d10. If the result is 7 or higher, the consumable is not destroyed and can be used again.

- **Easy** [Targeted, Trap]
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Exceptional Craftsmanship** [Seasoned]
  This Consumable’s roll now has 11 dice instead of 7

- **Mindblowing** [Seasoned]
  Fascinated targets receive a -2 dice penalty to any rolls they make to resist any other Effects that you attempt to use on them.
  *Errata: Does not include this Effect; you cannot use this Enhancement to make it easier to re-fascinate them.*

- **Multi-Sensory**
  This Effect works as long as the target can perceive you through any sense.

- **No Contest** [Seasoned]
  If the Contested Outcome of your activation roll is 4 or higher, the activation cost of this Effect is refunded.

- **Quick n Dirty** [Trap]
  Placing a trap takes only a single Action.
  *Errata: Anyone who witnesses you place the trap learns about this type of trap and can avoid it at will.*

- **Quick n Dirty** [Trap]
  Placing this trap only takes a single Action.
  *Errata: Anyone who witnesses you place the trap learns about this type of trap and can avoid it at will.*

- **Self-Powered** [Targeted]
  Activating this Effect has no Exertion cost, but it can have no more than 3 Drawbacks and its Gift Cost can never be increased above 1.

- **Set it and Forget it** [Trap]
  Your traps last indefinitely. They can still be disarmed.

- **Specialized Snare** [Trap]
  When setting the trap, you determine the condition under which it triggers.
  *Errata: The trap's triggering condition, once set, is final. The trap cannot be manually triggered or made to fire on a selected target at will.*

- **Waste Not, Want Not** [Trap]
  If disarmed, this consumable trap may be collected and reused.
  *Errata: Triggered traps cannot be disarmed and recollected.*
  Detail required: Disarming Roll

- **Willful End**
  You may end this Effect before its duration has elapsed as a Free Action.

- **Would You Look at That?** [Veteran]
  You may opt to focus the target’s attention on any object or Being within range instead of yourself. If you do, you roll at +1 Difficulty.

- **You May Approach Me**
  Exert your Mind to compel any Fascinated targets to begin moving towards you.
  *Errata: This still does not change the target's opinion of you or their mood, so use with caution.*

## Drawbacks

- **Biological Psychology**
  This power only works on Sapient, Living targets

- **Conditional** [Targeted, Trap | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Cursed Item** [Trap]
  This consumable is an object that can be armed as a trap. The trap's trigger is using the object instead of entering the proximity of the trap. Ideal for cursed books, trapped phones, etc.
  *Errata: The trap effect's range is still measured from the object itself. The trigger must be an Action, not merely a glance (if the object is a mirror, the target must spend an action looking into the mirror)*

- **Disfiguring** [Targeted, Trap | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Disturbing**
  Witnessing this Effect is disturbing and/or morally abhorrent to the average person. Anyone who witnesses you during its activation or for its duration will be repulsed, outraged, and will likely think differently of you from that moment on.
  *Errata: Must go beyond the Effect's innate disturbing-ness. Examples include: transforming into a terrible monster, sacrificing a child, performing torture, etc. Playgroup leaders should account for what is "normal" in their chosen setting. Be cognizant of your group's tone and accommodating to those who would like to "fast forward" through the description.*
  Detail required: Disturbing aspect
  Requires: drawbacks: overt

- **Draining**
  After this Effect ends, you cannot move quicker than a walk (15 feet per Round) for one minute and suffer a -1 dice penalty for an hour.

- **Fan Dance**
  This Effect lasts as long as you maintain Concentration.

- **Fickle Traps** [Trap]
  Your traps last only an hour once placed.

- **Focus** [Targeted, Trap]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Fuel** [Targeted, Trap]
  Using this Effect consumes some material good in addition to its other costs.
  Detail required: Fuel Unit

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Mage**
  Activating this Effect requires two consecutive Actions of Concentration. It involves intricate use of the body and voice. If interrupted during activation, the Effect fails. It is obvious you are preparing to activate an Effect during casting.
  *Errata: Just about any attempt to interrupt the ritual should be successful.*
  Detail required: Activation Ritual

- **Once per Day** [Targeted, Trap]
  You may only use this Effect once per day.

- **Overt**
  This Effect is obviously Alien and eye-catching across its range to those witnessing it, and you are obviously the source.

- **Sabotage Item** [Trap]
  Instead of placing a trap at a fixed location, you may sabotage an object that can be carried or moved. The trap's trigger is using the object instead of entering the proximity of the trap. Ideal for poisons, trapping vehicles, cursing clothes, etc.
  *Errata: The trap effect's range is still measured from the object itself. The trigger must be an Action, not merely a glance (if the object is a mirror, the target must spend an action looking into the mirror)*

- **Sacrifice** [Targeted, Trap]
  Instead of Exerting your Mind or expending Source, activating this Effect gives a Severity-1 Injury that worsens by 1 Severity each time you use an Effect with Sacrifice. This Injury cannot be prevented by any means or healed before 1 day has passed.
  *Errata: This Injury does not require Stabilization or cause Battle Scars when it becomes Severe. It cannot be transferred using Energy Transfer.*

- **Traumatic** [Targeted, Trap]
  You must make a Trauma roll when you use this Effect. Its Difficulty cannot be reduced by any means. If you fail or Botch, you receive one Mind Damage and a new Trauma.

- **Unreliable**
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
