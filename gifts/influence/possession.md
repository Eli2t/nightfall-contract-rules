# Possession

*Take over another's body*

**Category:** Influence | **Required Status:** Seasoned

**Vectors:** Targeted

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Consumable Crafting, Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You are able to temporarily transfer your consciousness into someone, possessing them.  They remain aware but helpless during the ordeal. 

Forcing them to perform actions that violate their most deeply-held convictions makes it easier for them to force you out. 

If the body you are possessing or the body you left behind dies, you die.

## Enhancements

- **Ammo**
  This consumable is meant to be used as ammo in a ranged weapon, applied to a melee weapon as poison, or to be thrown. It uses the standard attack roll and range for the selected attack type. The attack itself does no damage, but the Effect may.
  *Errata: Attack types include: firearms, thrown, bow-and-arrow, sling, etc. The Attack roll used to activate this Consumable cannot be at a Difficulty lower than 6. You may only use one Consumable per Action.*
  Detail required: Attack type

- **Autopilot**
  While possessing your target, you may put them into “autopilot.” During this time they will act as they would normally for the situation without the knowledge that you are/were possessing them.
  *Errata: This allows them to act “normal” around social contacts, type in passwords if they want to log in, etc. You have no control during this time.*

- **Battery-operated** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Instead of Exerting Mind to activate this Effect, it has its own 3-point Source pool. The Source regenerates at the rate of one point per day and cannot be raised with Experience.
  *Errata: Different Effects on the same Artifact with this Enhancement share a battery pool. Each additional Battery-Operated after the first increases the capacity of the Source by 3, to a maximum of 12.*

- **Black-Out**
  The target has no recollection of what happened while Possessed. Any Traumas they acquired during the affair remain.

- **Climb In**
  You don’t leave a body behind while this Effect is active.

- **Demonic Possession** [Veteran]
  You have access to the target's Powers while possessing them.

- **Durable** [Consumable Crafting]
  When you use this Consumable, roll a single d10. If the result is 7 or higher, the consumable is not destroyed and can be used again.

- **Easy** [Targeted]
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Emergency Recall**
  If the possessed body dies during the duration, you are returned to your original body instead of dying.

- **Exceptional Craftsmanship** [Seasoned]
  This Consumable’s roll now has 11 dice instead of 7

- **Ghost In The Machine**
  You may now possess any Animate target.

- **Hitchhiker**
  If you would die because the possession ends and your original body was destroyed, make a contested Mind roll against the target. The loser becomes a helpless observer as long as you both inhabit their body.
  *Errata: If you are a helpless observer, you cannot activate any Effects, including this one, with one exception: If a co-inhabited body is possessed, a helpless observer may cast Possession on the new inhabitant's original body.*

- **Immortal Spirit**
  If you die from the destruction of your body, your disembodied spirit remains "alive" for one hour, fixed to the location of your corpse. During this time, the only Action you can take is to activate this Power on a target in range.

- **Muscle Memory**
  Your Ability ratings are your own or those of the creature you are possessing, whichever is higher.

- **No Contest** [Seasoned]
  If the Contested Outcome of your activation roll is 4 or higher, the activation cost of this Effect is refunded.

- **Open Book** [Veteran]
  You may access the knowledge and memories of the creature you are possessing

- **Self-Powered** [Targeted]
  Activating this Effect has no Exertion cost, but it can have no more than 3 Drawbacks and its Gift Cost can never be increased above 1.

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Targeted | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Contest of Will**
  The possessed creature may Exert their Mind to kick you out of their body. You may Exert your Mind to counteract being kicked out.
  *Errata: This process is repeatable as many times as needed. But the Target may believe they have kicked you out after the first time.*

- **Critical Failure**
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Disturbing** [Targeted]
  Witnessing this Effect's Activation is extremely disturbing and/or morally abhorrent to the average person. Anyone who witnesses its activation will almost certainly be repulsed, outraged, and think differently of you from that moment on.
  *Errata: Must go beyond the Effect's innate disturbing-ness. Examples include: transforming into a terrible monster, sacrificing a child, performing torture, etc. Playgroup leaders should account for what is "normal" in their chosen setting. Be cognizant of your group's tone and accommodating to those who would like to "fast forward" through the description.*
  Detail required: Disturbing aspect
  Requires: drawbacks: overt, pact-of-power

- **Draining**
  After this Effect ends, you cannot move quicker than a walk (15 feet per Round) for one minute and suffer a -1 dice penalty for an hour.

- **Easy to Resist**
  The target must either consent to be targeted or be unconscious, hypnotized, thoroughly drugged, in surgery or similarly incapacitated such that they could not or would not roll to resist this Power.
  *Errata: If this condition is satisfied, no defensive rolls may be made by the target.*

- **Focus** [Targeted]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Fuel** [Targeted]
  Using this Effect consumes some material good in addition to its other costs.
  Detail required: Fuel Unit

- **Get Out!**
  There is some common occurrence that causes you to be ejected from your inhabited body.
  *Errata: The event must be relatively common or simple (flash of light, splashed by water, etc)*
  Detail required: Event

- **Left Behind**
  You do not have access to your Active Powers while possessing.

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Mage**
  Activating this Effect requires two consecutive Actions of Concentration. It involves intricate use of the body and voice. If interrupted during activation, the Effect fails. It is obvious you are preparing to activate an Effect during casting.
  *Errata: Just about any attempt to interrupt the ritual should be successful.*
  Detail required: Activation Ritual

- **Mind Swap**
  You switch places with the target you’re possessing instead of simply leaving your body behind. You will be unable to use your original body's senses during this time.

- **Once per Day** [Targeted]
  You may only use this Effect once per day.

- **Passenger**
  You're just along for the ride: you have no control over the actions of your host, just access to their senses. You may Exert your Mind to force them to take an Action.

- **Power Struggle**
  The target may Exert their Mind at any time. If they do, they may choose the next Action they take.
  *Errata: This Action is taken instead of your own. If in combat, the target may Exert their Mind at any time, regardless of imitative. This cannot be used to "cancel" an Action the possessor has already declared.*

- **Sacrifice** [Targeted]
  Instead of Exerting your Mind or expending Source, activating this Effect gives a Severity-1 Injury that worsens by 1 Severity each time you use an Effect with Sacrifice. This Injury cannot be prevented by any means or healed before 1 day has passed.
  *Errata: This Injury does not require Stabilization or cause Battle Scars when it becomes Severe. It cannot be transferred using Energy Transfer.*

- **Super Shroom**
  If you become Incapacitated or suffer an Injury Severity 4 or greater, this Effect ends.

- **Sympathetic Pain**
  If the possessed body takes any damage, you do as well.

- **Traumatic** [Targeted]
  You must make a Trauma roll when you use this Effect. Its Difficulty cannot be reduced by any means. If you fail or Botch, you receive one Mind Damage and a new Trauma.

- **Tunnel Vision**
  While this Effect is active, you are at -2 dice to all Actions and cannot Concentrate.

- **What's Wrong**
  Your inhabited body undergoes a physical and/or behavioral change for the duration of your possession that makes it obvious that something is seriously amiss.
  *Errata: The change can be either physical or behavioral, but it must be immediately obvious either way.*
  Detail required: Indicator
