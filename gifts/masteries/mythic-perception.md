# Mythic Perception

*Be extremely observant*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

Your awareness and discernment are the stuff of legends.  You rarely fail at actions which depend on these traits, and have access to an array of relevant effects.

## Enhancements

- **Clear-Sighted**
  If you see an illusion, disguise, or other false reality, you recognize it as such but cannot immediately see through it.

- **Compensate**
  If any of your senses are suppressed, they may still be used for rolls at +2 Difficulty.

- **Constant Vigilance**
  You may React to surprise Attacks, and you do not automatically lose initiative if ambushed or caught-flat footed. +2 dice to Initiative.
  *Errata: You can still be ambushed if you are incapacitated, stunned, or being kept unconscious supernaturally.*

- **Gut Feeling**
  You may roll Perception + Alertness Difficulty 7 to detect when someone is watching you remotely, even through supernatural means. Requires Outcome 4 or higher.

- **Saw it Coming**
  Any time you attempt to dodge an Attack from someone lower than your position in Initiative, you may Exert your Mind to automatically succeed without a roll. You may use Source to make a Desperate Defense.
  *Errata: There is still a maximum of one Desperate Defense per Round*

- **Seeing Double**
  The effective range of all your senses are multiplied by your rating in Extra Perception + 1. This does not allow you to ignore environmental factors and excess noise.
  *Errata: The additional sensory input should never decrease the effectiveness of a sense. For senses without explicit ranges, GMs should consider how far a normal person could sense the input given current conditions and then multiply by that amount. For example, if you have to be within 5 feet to hear someone shouting your name in a noisy nightclub, multiply that distance.*

- **Sherlock** [Seasoned]
  You may Exert your Mind to deduce a fragment of the chain of events that led to a particular perceivable detail of an individual or environment. This Effect reveals one piece of information that is not discoverable by a mundane investigation.
  *Errata: You may not use this Effect on the same target more than once in a 24 hour period. The amount of information received is limited at the GM's discretion but should at least contain some information that would not be learned from a mundane investigation.*

- **The Great Hunter** [Veteran]
  Once per Contract and once per Downtime, you may Exert your Mind and spend one minute activating this Effect to learn the location of any being or object you have an intuitive understanding of.
  *Errata: The location given by the GM should be an area no smaller than a city block.*

## Drawbacks

- **Can't Unsee**
  Trauma rolls are at +2 Difficulty.

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Samson**
  A marking or physical trait is the source of your power. Losing it removes all benefits for the next 2 months. If someone witnesses you using this Effect, they get a Perception check to determine the source of your power.
  Detail required: Source of your Power

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
