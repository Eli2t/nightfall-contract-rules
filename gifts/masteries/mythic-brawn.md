# Mythic Brawn

*Be very hardy*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

Your strength and stamina are the stuff of legends. You rarely fail at actions which depend on these traits, and have access to an array of relevant effects

## Enhancements

- **Absorb** [Seasoned]
  If you are aware of an incoming source of Damage, you may Exert your Mind and spend a Reaction to reduce all Damage from that source to 0.
  *Errata: “Source of Damage” refers to an Attack, environmental obstacle, Effect, etc. Only prevents one instance of Damage.*

- **Atlas** [Veteran]
  You may take a Severity-1 Injury to ignore your lifting and hauling limits for a single, non-Combat Action. For example, you may uproot a tree, lift a large truck, or similar. This injury heals in 24 hours and cannot be reduced or healed by any means

- **Beast of Burden**
  You may now carry up to your lifting and hauling capacity without suffering an encumbrance penalty or needing to Concentrate. You do not tire from extended physical activity.

- **Champion of the Caber Toss** [Seasoned]
  You may lift an additional 2000 pounds times your rating in Extra Brawn, and you may throw items up to this weight to a distance of your throwing roll’s Outcome x10 feet.
  *Errata: Weapon Damage for a heavy improvised thrown weapon is equal to the Brawn required to lift it minus 2 (minimum 0).*

- **Gains Before Brains**
  You may spend an Action and recover any amount of Mind Damage. Take an Injury equal to the amount of Mind Damage recovered that cannot be reduced by any means or healed within one day.

- **Heavy Hitter**
  Successful attacks made with Brawn will either knock your target back Outcome x 5 feet, or knock them down to the ground.  Choose which effect the attack will have prior to rolling for it.
  *Errata: If a character is knocked down, standing up will impose a -2 penalty on their next action.*

- **High Constitution**
  You gain +2 dice to any Body resistance rolls. Any failed or botched Body rolls will instead give an outcome of 1.

- **Immovable Object**
  As long as you are standing still on a solid surface, you may Exert your Mind and spend a Free Action to become immovable. As long as you maintain Concentration, you cannot be moved and are immune to Damage from collisions with larger objects.

- **Indomitable** [Novice]
  The Severity of all Injuries and Battle Scars you receive is reduced by 1. Will to Survive can reduce an incoming Injury’s Severity by up to 10 levels, to a minimum of 5.
  *Errata: This Injury Severity reduction does not apply to Injuries or Battle Scars suffered as a cost for an Effect, and it does not stack with Will to Survive’s reductions.*

- **Tough** [Novice]
  You gain Armor equal to 2 plus your rating in Extra Brawn. This Armor cannot be penetrated or shredded and protects against falling Damage.
  *Errata: Armor from multiple sources does not stack. Instead, the highest is used.*

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Ham-Fisted**
  You cannot limit yourself when handling delicate objects or physically interacting with people. You may need to roll Dexterity + Athletics to avoid breaking things or causing unintended injury.
  *Errata: This also prevents the use of damage limiting mechanics in combat.*

- **Heavy**
  You weigh at least 2500 pounds. All rolls to swim, jump, climb, or fly are made at +2 Difficulty. Many vehicles, structures, and similar have trouble supporting your weight and might break or not operate properly.

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Samson**
  A marking or physical trait is the source of your power. Losing it removes all benefits for the next 2 months. If someone witnesses you using this Effect, they get a Perception check to determine the source of your power.
  Detail required: Source of your Power

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
