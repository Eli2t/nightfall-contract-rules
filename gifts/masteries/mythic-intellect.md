# Mythic Intellect

*Be very smart*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

Your logic and reasoning are the stuff of legends. You rarely fail at actions which depend on these traits, and have access to an array of relevant effects.

## Enhancements

- **Hypothetically Speaking. . .** [Seasoned]
  Once per Contract you may Exert your Mind to ask the GM question of the form “would X happen if Y?” and receive an answer of “yes,” “no,” “probably,” or “probably not”.
  *Errata: For example, you could ask “would it be bad for my health if I touch the magic orb?” Or “would I be able to issue a self-destruct order to the space ship if I found the control panel?” The GM must answer with as much certainty as they can.*

- **Multithreaded** [Seasoned]
  You may take Actions and Reactions at a -4 dice penalty without disrupting your Concentration. You can maintain concentration on two things simultaneously, but you are unable to take Actions or Reactions while doing so.

- **Never Forget**
  You have a perfect photographic memory, and can recall or recreate even complex and detailed things.  If you forget something you had previously seen or noticed, you may ask the GM and they will remind you.

- **Prodigy**
  Whenever you witness someone get an Outcome of 6 or higher, or someone demonstrates an Ability with the intent of teaching you, you may Exert your Mind. If you do, rolls using that Ability use their rating instead of your own for the next day.

- **Push Through It**
  When you Exert your Mind to ignore Stress and gain +1 to your Outcome, it lasts for 3 Rounds.

- **Quick Thinking**
  Any Intellect rolls other than Effect activations are made as Free Actions. You get +2 dice to all Initiative rolls.

- **Remarkably Self-Aware**
  You may roll Intellect at Difficulty 6 to “scan” yourself for abnormalities. With Outcome 1-3, any outside influences on your thoughts/mind will be detected, as well as Traumas. With Outcome 4+, you may Exert your Mind to cancel the Effect.
  *Errata: One attempt per Effect. The GM may make this roll secretly for you if necessary.*

- **Sheer Willpower** [Seasoned]
  You may Exert your Mind up to three times per Round, including multiple times for each roll.
  *Errata: The Outcome bonus from the Enhancement Push Through It is limited to +1.*

- **Utmost Efficiency** [Veteran]
  Any time you Exert your Mind, you may roll 1d10 Difficulty 6. If you succeed, your exertion does not cause Mind damage

- **Wait For Your Moment**
  You may take your Action on the turn of anyone who has a lower Initiative than you. If you do, you receive +2 dice to that roll.

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Insufferable Know-It-All**
  Any social rolls you make are at +1 Difficulty, and if someone else can answer an informational question or solve a puzzle before you do, make a Trauma roll.
  *Errata: The Difficulty of this Trauma roll cannot be reduced by any means.*

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Samson**
  A marking or physical trait is the source of your power. Losing it removes all benefits for the next 2 months. If someone witnesses you using this Effect, they get a Perception check to determine the source of your power.
  Detail required: Source of your Power

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
