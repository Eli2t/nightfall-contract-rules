# Unarmed Mastery

*Punch, kick, and wrestle your foes*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

This Power grants access to an array of powers relating to the usage of unarmed combat.

## Enhancements

- **Accursed Wounds**
  Any Injury inflicted is considered Unstabilized no matter its Severity. The roll required to Stabilize it is treated as though it is a minimum of Severity 6. These wounds do not heal naturally, though Effects may still be used to heal them.

- **Assassin**
  If your attack Injures your target, they cannot make a sound, even incidentally, for a single Round.
  *Errata: Even the sound of their body and equipment dropping to the ground is silenced.*

- **Body Slam**
  If you move and then make a successful attack against a target with a Brawn rating less than or equal to yours, if your target was closer than the full distance you could have moved, you may knock them back the excess movement distance.
  *Errata: You may choose whether or not to follow the target you knocked back.*

- **Bodyguard**
  You may use your Reaction or a Desperate Defense to Defend against any Attack where the attacker, target, or line of fire is within 15 feet. If you do so, you may opt to take the Damage from the Attack instead of the target.

- **Brutal Strikes**
  If your Attack inflicts a Battle Scar, you may choose which Battle Scar of the appropriate level the target suffers. If it causes a less severe Injury, you may inflict a Minor Battle Scar of your choosing.

- **Called it**
  Called Shots that you attempt only require a Contested Outcome of 2 for small targets and 4 for tiny targets.

- **Disarm**
  If you successfully Defend or Clash with a melee attack, you may take possession your opponent’s weapon or launch if up to 35 feet away.

- **Fight Circle** [Seasoned]
  Mob Outcome bonuses do not apply to Attacks or Grapples against you within melee range. Any Attack or Grapple made against you after the first each Round is made at a -3 dice penalty.

- **Finesse**
  When Attacking a single being, you may limit the Severity of any Injury you inflict or determine a maximum Wound Level, including Incapacitated. The Injury is treated as normal for Battle Scars and Stabilization

- **Flow Like Water**
  If you win a Clash, you may redirect the incoming Attack to another target within its original range using your Clash Outcome as its own. You may Clash against an Attack as long as the attacker, target, or line of fire is within arm’s reach.
  *Errata: If you fail a Clash on behalf of another target, the target may choose to React as normal to the original Attack.*

- **Ghost Wrangler**
  You may grapple incorporeal, liquid, or gaseous entities.

- **Jiu-Jitsu**
  When you successfully start or maintain a Grapple, you may choose to deal Damage to the target equal to the Grapple’s Contested Outcome.
  *Errata: Additional Damage from the Additional Damage Parameter does not apply to Damage dealt through this Enhancement.*

- **Meat Shield**
  If you have successfully grappled a target, you may use a Reaction to redirect any incoming attack to them instead of you.

- **One Inch Punch**
  You may make a full attack against any target you can touch, even if you are restrained. To an observer, you hardly move. In a crowded area, determining that you were the source of an attack requires a Perception + Alertness roll with an Outcome of 4
  *Errata: You may make a full attack against any target you can touch, even if you are restrained. To an observer, you hardly move. In a crowded area, determining that you were the source of an attack requires a Perception + Alertness roll with an Outcome of 4.*

- **Precision Strikes**
  You may use Dexterity instead of Brawn to Attack and Defend. If you would successfully incapacitate a target using Finesse, you may opt to knock them out for one hour instead of having them suffer an Injury.
  Requires: enhancements: finesse

- **Shredder**
  If you successfully hit your target, the value of any Armor they are wearing is reduced by 2. For material armor, this penalty lasts until it is repaired. For intrinsic armor, it lasts until the end of Combat and any relevant wound is healed.

- **Transcendence** [Seasoned]
  When Defending, you may Exert your Mind to Defend against any number of incoming Attacks for the rest of the Round.
  *Errata: When Defending against a mob, you do not suffer the Difficulty Penalty, and their Attack does not gain the normal Outcome bonus.*

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Focus** [Passive]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Grabby**
  This mastery does not apply to your standard unarmed attacks, only grapples and chokes.

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
