# Mythic Charisma

*Be extremely charming*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

Your presence and personality are the stuff of legends.  You rarely fail at actions which depend on these traits, and have access to an array of relevant effects.

## Enhancements

- **Anime**
  You may have a 30 second conversation with another character on your initiative each Round in Combat, and using any Communication Effect takes a Free Action instead of a full Action.

- **Call me "Senpai"**
  You may ask to be called by a specific title. Sapient beings will use this title when addressing you, unless they Exert their Mind to resist for the day. You may use an Influence Effect on anyone who uses your title without Exerting your Mind.
  *Errata: For example: "My lord"  or "Bro". One free activation per individual per day.*
  Detail required: Name and Gesture

- **Come Again?**
  Whenever you say something or present yourself in a way that upsets someone, you may Exert your Mind. If you do so you may change what you said or how you present yourself, and the first attempt will be dismissed and forgotten.

- **Exploit**
  Any non-Effect influence roll against a target is made at -1 Difficulty for each Trauma of theirs that you know about. Effect rolls are capped at -1 Difficulty.

- **Hey, Listen!**
  You cannot be ignored or unheard when directly addressing an entity.

- **Honest Eyes**
  Your word is accepted as honest so long as you are speaking the truth as you understand it. You can always tell if someone telling a lie without making a roll, though you may not know what they are concealing.

- **Impeccable Judgement** [Veteran]
  Once per Contract and Downtime, you may ask one (out of character) yes/no question about someone you are conversing with. The person playing the target must answer truthfully.
  *Errata: This does not count as an Effect, Alien Investigation, or similar and cannot be resisted.*

- **Life-Changing Conversation** [Seasoned]
  Speak to a target for 10 minutes, Exert your Mind, and roll Charisma + Influence, Difficulty of the target’s Mind score. If you achieve a complete success, you may suppress one of their Limits or add a new Limit. Lasts two months.

- **Me, an Empath**
  You have an intuitive understanding of the emotional state of any Animate being you're aware of within 30 feet. Once per person per day you may roll Charisma + Influence to learn what things they respect or disrespect in others.
  *Errata: On its own, this does not allow you to detect lies.*

- **No Choice But To Stan**
  If someone speaks or acts against you socially, anyone observing it must roll Mind, with the difficulty equal to your Charisma, in order to not defend you.
  *Errata: Being forced to Stan will not force someone to violate any of their Limits or to engage in violence of any kind (though they may still do so of their own volition).*

- **Party Animal**
  You are welcome at, and will be granted entrance to, any social gathering or party, at least as an observer.

- **Poker Face**
  You reveal only what you want to reveal. It is impossible to determine whether or not you are lying. You may roll Charisma + Performance to resist any investigative Effect that targets you.

- **Sensei**
  You may Exert your Mind and spend 1 minute teaching an Ability to up to 5 people. Roll Charisma + Performance, Difficulty 7. For the next three hours, their rating in that Ability is increased by your Outcome to a maximum of your rating in it.

- **Unflappable**
  Any attempts to control or influence your thoughts or emotions are rolled at +1 Difficulty per Extra Charisma.
  *Errata: Warning: If you reduce your Charisma bonus down to 0, this Enhancement will do nothing.*

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Samson**
  A marking or physical trait is the source of your power. Losing it removes all benefits for the next 2 months. If someone witnesses you using this Effect, they get a Perception check to determine the source of your power.
  Detail required: Source of your Power

- **The Price of Fame**
  Any sightings or recordings of your activity can easily be traced back to you. Even when disguised or using an alias, people always get a Perception check to recognize you.

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
