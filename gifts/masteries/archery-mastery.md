# Archery Mastery

*Become a pro with your bow*

**Category:** Masteries

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

This Power grants access to an array of powers relating to the usage of bows and arrows (but not crossbows, those are considered a firearm due to the trigger mechanism).

The Difficulty to fire bows is reduced to 6.

## Enhancements

- **All-Terrain Archer**
  Your attacks never suffer penalties due to movement or environmental conditions. This includes sprinting, climbing, firing in the rain, etc.

- **Arc the Arrow** [Seasoned]
  You suffer no penalties for attacking targets behind cover.
  *Errata: The arrow should have to make a maximum of a single turn to hit its target. It cannot traverse an arbitrarily complex path.*

- **Assassin**
  If your attack Injures your target, they cannot make a sound, even incidentally, for a single Round.
  *Errata: Even the sound of their body and equipment dropping to the ground is silenced.*

- **Blood Conduit** [Seasoned]
  Any Injury caused by one of your projectiles satisfies the Conduit requirement for any other Powers you possess. This lasts until the Injury is healed or utilized as a Conduit.

- **Bullet Parry**
  You may use a Reaction to Defend against any Melee, thrown, firearm, or projectile Attack in range of your Attacks.

- **Bullseye**
  If you are not engaged in Combat or activating an Effect, any projectile you fire is considered a complete success without needing to make a roll.

- **Called it**
  Called Shots that you attempt only require a Contested Outcome of 2 for small targets and 4 for tiny targets.

- **Finesse**
  When Attacking a single being, you may limit the Severity of any Injury you inflict or determine a maximum Wound Level, including Incapacitated. The Injury is treated as normal for Battle Scars and Stabilization

- **Hail Storm**
  You may Exert your Mind to make an attack against all targets within a 120 degree arc in front of you. Your full attack Outcome applies to each of them, but they may attempt to Dodge or Defend if they are aware of the attack.

- **Homing Arrow** [Veteran]
  You may Exert your Mind to make a single attack against any target in range without penalty, regardless of cover, as long as there is a path the projectile could take to hit them.
  Requires: enhancements: arc-arrow, i-shoot-my-mind

- **I Shoot with my Mind**
  You may Exert your Mind to fire a shot completely blind without penalty.

- **Improvised Ammo**
  You may use any object that is vaguely the same size and shape as your weapon’s ammo in place of its normal ammo with no penalty.

- **Payload**
  Your projectiles can carry a payload of up to 3 lbs without a penalty.

- **Pin Down**
  If one of your Attacks injures a target near a wall or floor, they are pinned to that surface. Safely removing the projectile costs a Quick Action. If they move before removing it, the Injury’s Severity worsens by 1.

- **Pincushion**
  Any attack or Effect activation roll against a target that already has one of your projectiles stuck into them is rolled at -1 Difficulty.

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Specialist**
  These benefits only apply to a certain specific class of weaponry.
  *Errata: Examples: automatic rifles, six-shooters, knives, batons, Russian manufactured weapons, etc*
  Detail required: Type of Weapon

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
