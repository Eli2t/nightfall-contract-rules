# Flight

*Rise above it all*

**Category:** Mobility

**Vectors:** Activated, Passive

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Consumable Crafting, Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You are capable of flying, levitating, or otherwise moving in a manner that defies gravity.

## Enhancements

- **Battery-operated** [Activated | Stackable]
  Instead of Exerting Mind to activate this Effect, it has its own 3-point Source pool. The Source regenerates at the rate of one point per day and cannot be raised with Experience.
  *Errata: Different Effects on the same Artifact with this Enhancement share a battery pool. Each additional Battery-Operated after the first increases the capacity of the Source by 3, to a maximum of 12.*

- **Cargo Carrier** [Seasoned]
  Using your wings, you can lift 500 pounds more than you’d normally be able to. You may fly at a minimum of 5 feet per Round when over-encumbered as long as you are below your lifting and hauling capacity.
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight; drawbacks: bulky-wings

- **Conditionally Costless** [Activated | Seasoned]
  This Effect does not require Exertion in certain circumstances. The condition should be balanced such that it has an activation cost the majority of the time you are able to use it.
  *Errata: If you take this Enhancement, you must be able to use the Effect at full cost and other times at no cost. 

This cannot be the same condition as the Drawback "Conditional" as that would imply that the Effect is costless any time you can cast it.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Dive Bomb**
  When falling, you may rapidly accelerate beyond terminal velocity, moving 2000 feet per round, so long as you fall straight down, allowing you to easily catch other falling targets.

- **Durable** [Consumable Crafting]
  When you use this Consumable, roll a single d10. If the result is 7 or higher, the consumable is not destroyed and can be used again.

- **Easy** [Activated]
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Ethereal Wings** [Seasoned]
  The feature of your body which enables flight can no longer be damaged, destroyed, or receive Battle Scars.
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Evasive Maneuvers**
  During your turn, if you moved mostly through the air but traveled less than your Free Movement, all attacks against you are at a -2 dice penalty until your next turn.

- **Feathery Sanctuary** [Seasoned]
  You may use your wings as a shield. Spend a Quick Action to wrap yourself in your wings. During this time, you have a shield that does not require a hand and provides +3 Armor and a -3 dice penalty to physical actions. You may stop at any time.
  *Errata: While shielding, you cannot fly and will fall if airborne. You may end shielding if needed to catch yourself from taking a hard impact*
  Requires: enhancements: ethereal-wings

- **Goshawk** [Seasoned]
  You don't require extra clear space on any side in order to fly, and get +2 dice for any rolls made to maneuver in mid-air
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Gust** [Seasoned]
  You may spend an Action and roll Brawn + Athletics to create a powerful gust of wind covering a 35 x 35 foot area in front of you. Affected targets may be disarmed, knocked down, thrown back, and take Damage.
  *Errata: Affected targets are knocked down if your Outcome is higher than their Dexterity, thrown back 30 feet if higher than their Brawn, disarmed if higher than their weapon’s Ability rating, and take Damage equal to your Outcome minus their Body score. Lightweight things may be thrown far or broken at the GM’s discretion.*
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Magnificent Liftoff** [Seasoned]
  If you are restricted, restrained, dazed, sleepy, or stunned, activating this Effect immediately ends the condition. Cooldown 1 minute.
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Piece of Cake** [Seasoned]
  You may activate this Effect as a Free Action, meaning you may activate it and take an Action in the same Round of Combat.
  *Errata: A Free Action is one that can be taken at any time without spending the Round's Action.  Activation costs (Exertion, etc) are unaffected.*

- **Pixie Dust** [Seasoned | Stackable]
  You may grant flight and fly with an additional person at no penalty. Those whom you grant flight to can fly at your speed if touching you or Mind x5 feet/Round if on their own. If they do not touch you for two Rounds, they fall.
  Requires: enhancements: telekinetic-flight

- **Space Cadet** [Seasoned]
  This Effect is usable even in a complete vacuum. It does not give you the ability to breathe or survive in a vacuum. While flying through a vacuum, you may slowly accelerate to near light speed, allowing you to traverse space, given enough time.
  Requires: enhancements: telekinetic-flight

- **Take Wing** [Seasoned]
  You may ascend into the air and fly using some physical feature of your body.
  Detail required: Physical Feature

- **Telekinetic Flight** [Seasoned]
  You may ascend into the air and fly using your mind.

- **Torpedo** [Seasoned]
  You may use your flight underwater.
  Requires: enhancements: telekinetic-flight

## Drawbacks

- **Bulky Wings**
  You require 10 feet of clear space on all sides to take off and ascend into the air.
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Clumsy Flight**
  Any roll to land or maneuver in the air is rolled at +2 Difficulty. When you fail or botch, take a Severity-1 Injury.

- **Conditional** [Activated, Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Activated, Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Disturbing** [Activated]
  Witnessing this Effect is disturbing and/or morally abhorrent to the average person. Anyone who witnesses you during its activation or for its duration will be repulsed, outraged, and will likely think differently of you from that moment on.
  *Errata: Must go beyond the Effect's innate disturbing-ness. Examples include: transforming into a terrible monster, sacrificing a child, performing torture, etc. Playgroup leaders should account for what is "normal" in their chosen setting. Be cognizant of your group's tone and accommodating to those who would like to "fast forward" through the description.*
  Detail required: Disturbing aspect
  Requires: drawbacks: overt

- **Draining**
  After this Effect ends, you cannot move quicker than a walk (15 feet per Round) for one minute and suffer a -1 dice penalty for an hour.

- **Focus** [Activated, Passive]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Fuel** [Activated]
  Using this Effect consumes some material good in addition to its other costs.
  Detail required: Fuel Unit

- **Icarus**
  If you fly higher than 50 feet in the air, you must roll 1 die, Difficulty 6. If you fail, this Effect ends early and cannot be reactivated for an hour. Make this roll again at every interval of 50 feet off the ground (100 feet, 150 feet, etc).

- **Long Cast**
  This Effect takes one minute to activate.

- **Look Ma, No Hands**
  You can’t use equipment with your hands while in the air.
  Requires: enhancements: take-wing-flight

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Mage**
  Activating this Effect requires two consecutive Actions of Concentration. It involves intricate use of the body and voice. If interrupted during activation, the Effect fails. It is obvious you are preparing to activate an Effect during casting.
  *Errata: Just about any attempt to interrupt the ritual should be successful.*
  Detail required: Activation Ritual

- **Once per Day** [Activated]
  You may only use this Effect once per day.

- **Sacrifice** [Activated]
  Instead of Exerting your Mind or expending Source, activating this Effect gives a Severity-1 Injury that worsens by 1 Severity each time you use an Effect with Sacrifice. This Injury cannot be prevented by any means or healed before 1 day has passed.
  *Errata: This Injury does not require Stabilization or cause Battle Scars when it becomes Severe. It cannot be transferred using Energy Transfer.*

- **Stuck in the Sky**
  You cannot end this Effect early and cannot come closer than 15 feet to the ground until the effect ends.
  Requires: enhancements: telekinetic-flight

- **Traumatic** [Activated]
  You must make a Trauma roll when you use this Effect. Its Difficulty cannot be reduced by any means. If you fail or Botch, you receive one Mind Damage and a new Trauma.

- **Tunnel Vision**
  While this Effect is active, you are at -2 dice to all Actions and cannot Concentrate.

- **Unreliable**
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
