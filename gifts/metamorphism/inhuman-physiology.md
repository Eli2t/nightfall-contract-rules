# Inhuman Physiology

*Upgrade your body*

**Category:** Metamorphism

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power

## Description

Your body has been transformed or augmented to become beneficially inhuman in some way.  Your specific mutation must be physically visible.

## Enhancements

- **Able-bodied** [Stackable]
  Your body has a beneficial inhuman adaptation. This can provide up to +3 dice to a specific non-Attack Action (swimming, lockpicking, etc)
  *Errata: Any benefit you gain from this enhancement must be tied to an actual physical alteration.

A chosen Action must be specific, not general. Things like "movement" or "being badass" are unacceptable.*
  Detail required: Chosen Action

- **Bundled Up**
  You are immune to Exposure from cold. If cold is a substantial component of any Damage you would take (e.g. falling from a great distance into a vat of liquid nitrogen), that Damage is reduced by 2.

- **Distributed Systems**
  No single organ in your body is critical for life. Called Shots do no additional Damage to you. You may perceive the world through separated body parts, take Actions with them, and reattach them by spending an Action and Exerting your Mind.
  *Errata: Detached parts move at 5 feet per Round. You may activate awareness Effects through detached sensory organs. This does not grant extra Actions. Detaching a body part always causes a Battle Scar, and the detached part "dies" if the Battle Scar is healed.*

- **Got No Life**
  You are considered Non-Living, and your Injuries no longer degrade with time. You do not age naturally, and supernatural attempts to age you fail.
  *Errata: Powers which can only affect Living things have no effect on you. You must still perform some equivalent of eating, sleeping, and drinking unless other Effects allow you to ignore that requirement.*

- **Inferno Home** [Seasoned]
  You are immune to Exposure from heat. If heat  is a substantial component of any Damage you would take (e.g. slashed with a flaming sword), that Damage is reduced by 2.

- **Natural Environment** [Stackable]
  There is a particular hostile environment that you are uniquely suited to survive in. Heat, cold, pressure, radiation, and other native hazards do not affect you. Dangerous local flora and fauna will not bother you unless you give them a reason.
  Detail required: Environment

- **Physical Perfection** [Veteran]
  You get +3 to your Body rating, but -1 to your Mind rating.

- **Reach**
  Your appendages have 30 feet of reach.
  *Errata: You may stretch, retract, or move your limbs a great distance once per Round.*

- **Squishy**
  Your body can stretch and compress much more than normal, allowing you to squeeze through cracks and passageways a cat could fit through.

- **Tools at Hand** [Stackable]
  Your body provides you with the functionality of a particular set of mundane tools. If used to attack, these "tools" use the same stats as a knife.
  *Errata: For example: ”medical equipment” or “carpentry tools.” Cannot cover more tools than would fit in a regular backpack (27 Liters). You cannot use this Enhancement to satisfy the requirements of the Focus Drawback, nor can the tools be shared. Subject to Playgroup Leader approval.*
  Detail required: Tools

- **Tooth and Claw** [Stackable]
  You may make a particular kind of attack without any equipment that deals +2 Weapon Damage and behaves like a certain standard weapon. E.g. you may fire bullets from your palm, use your leg as a sword, or brawl ferociously with your claws.
  Detail required: Attack Type

- **Toxic Body**
  Any Animate being that consumes or is injected with your bodily fluids receives a Severity-1 Injury. Every Round, they must roll Body Difficulty 9. If they fail, the Injury worsens by 1 Severity. If they succeed, the Injury stops worsening.

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Focus** [Passive]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Hackable**
  You count as a Computer when targeted by Effects. You cannot ignore or decrease the effects of Stress for any social rolls you make.

- **Indiscriminate Killer**
  Whenever you enter Combat, roll Self-Control. If you fail, you cannot choose which targets to attack and cannot stop fighting until there are no targets remaining.

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Misshapen**
  You cannot be mistaken for a normal human. You cannot always use standard human clothes, equipment, vehicles, and facilities, and when you can, you suffer an increased Difficulty.

- **Mute**
  You cannot speak any coherent verbal language and must resort to other means of communication.

- **Paws**
  You are incapable of fine object manipulation and can only hold or grasp things in a crude, clumsy way.

- **Tamable**
  You count as a Creature when targeted by Effects. You cannot ignore or decrease the effects of Stress for any Self-Control rolls you make.
  *Errata: You still also count as Sapient.*

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*

- **Vulnerable** [Stackable]
  You are particularly weak to a particular kind of damage. All Injuries caused by damage of that type have their Severity increased by two.
  *Errata: Damage type examples include: Fire and heat, ice and cold, attacks made by cold iron, magic, etc.*
  Detail required: Damage Type
