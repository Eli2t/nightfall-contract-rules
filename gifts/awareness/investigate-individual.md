# Investigate Individual

*Learn about someone*

**Category:** Awareness

**Vectors:** Targeted, Trap

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Consumable Crafting, Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You have an uncanny ability to learn things about individuals through conversation, careful examination, scanning, or some other means. Your results are limited to particular types of information.

## Enhancements

- **Alien Autopsy** [Seasoned]
  You determine the effect of any active Alien energy detected, and you get an idea as to the sorts of ways that latent Alien energy may be channeled by the target.
  Requires: enhancements: influences

- **Alien Origin**
  Determine if this target is Alien in origin or whether it has any Alien energy or outside influences. You receive information about the type of energy, including the name of the relevant Source pool or equivalent.

- **Ammo**
  This consumable is meant to be used as ammo in a ranged weapon, poison a blade, or be thrown. It uses the standard attack roll and range for the selected attack type. It may or may not deal standard damage in addition to its Effect, user's choice.
  *Errata: Attack types include: firearms, thrown, bow-and-arrow, sling, etc. The Attack roll used to activate this Consumable cannot be at a Difficulty lower than 6. You may only use one Consumable per Action.*
  Detail required: Attack type

- **Battery-operated** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Instead of Exerting Mind to activate this Effect, it has its own 3-point Source pool. The Source regenerates at the rate of one point per day and cannot be raised with Experience.
  *Errata: Different Effects on the same Artifact with this Enhancement share a battery pool. Each additional Battery-Operated after the first increases the capacity of the Source by 3, to a maximum of 12.*

- **Beast Whisperer**
  You may target any Creatures with this Power.

- **Birth Certificate**
  You learn the target's age, place of birth, and how foreign they are to their current location.

- **Cantrip** [Targeted]
  Activating this Effect has no Exertion cost, but it can have no more than 3 Drawbacks and its Gift Cost can never be increased above 2.

- **Conditionally Costless** [Targeted | Seasoned]
  This Effect does not require Exertion in certain circumstances. The condition should be balanced such that it has an activation cost the majority of the time you are able to use it.
  *Errata: If you take this Enhancement, you must be able to use the Effect at full cost and other times at no cost. 

This cannot be the same condition as the Drawback "Conditional" as that would imply that the Effect is costless any time you can cast it.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Conduit**
  You may use this Effect at unlimited range, but you require a token of your target to do so. One activation per token. The chosen token must be an object that the target actively created or that was in the target’s possession for at least 24 hours.
  *Errata: For example, a sample of the target’s blood, an object of sentimental value to the target, a sample of the target’s handwriting, a drop of the target’s blood, a corpse of someone the target killed, a piece of art created by the target,  etc.*
  Detail required: Token Type

- **Diagnostic**
  Determine any medical or physiological issues they have, as well as their species and a general overview of their biology.

- **Dream-Seeker**
  You learn where the target sleeps, how long they tend to sleep each night, whether or not they have nightmares, and what their nightmares tend to be about.

- **Durable** [Consumable Crafting]
  When you use this Consumable, roll a single d10. If the result is 7 or higher, the consumable is not destroyed and can be used again.

- **Easy** [Targeted, Trap]
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Exceptional Craftsmanship** [Seasoned]
  This Consumable’s roll now has 11 dice instead of 7

- **Killer**
  Determine how many living beings the target has killed, what sorts of creatures they were, and how long it has been since they last killed.

- **Lucifer**
  You learn a hidden desire of the target.
  *Errata: Roll Outcome determines the relevancy / secret-ness of the desire.*

- **Mental Health Check**
  You learn the target’s Traumas, Limits, and whether or not their thoughts, emotions, or memories are being influenced by any Alien forces.

- **Name**
  Learn the target's name, nicknames, screen names, and aliases

- **Other** [Stackable]
  You can learn some other piece of information about the target
  *Errata: Must be specific and relatively narrow in scope. Subject to Playgroup Leader approval for balance.*
  Detail required: Type of Information

- **Passwords**
  You learn the target’s passwords, passcodes, secret phrases, pin numbers, etc

- **Quick n Dirty** [Trap]
  Placing a trap takes only a single Action.
  *Errata: Anyone who witnesses you place the trap learns about this type of trap and can avoid it at will.*

- **Quick n Dirty** [Trap]
  Placing this trap only takes a single Action.
  *Errata: Anyone who witnesses you place the trap learns about this type of trap and can avoid it at will.*

- **Self-Powered** [Targeted]
  Activating this Effect has no Exertion cost, but it can have no more than 3 Drawbacks and its Gift Cost can never be increased above 1.

- **Set it and Forget it** [Trap]
  Your traps last indefinitely. They can still be disarmed.

- **Specialized Snare** [Trap]
  When setting the trap, you determine the condition under which it triggers.
  *Errata: The trap's triggering condition, once set, is final. The trap cannot be manually triggered or made to fire on a selected target at will.*

- **Them Digits**
  You learn how to reliably contact the target.
  *Errata: Low Outcome: a means of communication (phone, email, a secretary). High Outcome: specific phone numbers, email addresses, protocols for getting access to the individual.*

- **Tracking**
  The target’s trail for the past 24 hours is revealed to you and anyone you teach to follow it. This trail shows how long ago the target was there and is followable even when they are in a vehicle.
  *Errata: You are not made instantly aware of where they have been in the past 24 hours; you must follow their trail. You may "happen upon" any trail they have made in the past 24 hours.*

- **Treasure Map**
  You learn what the target values most and where to find it, provided they know. This includes any hidden or concealed treasures.

- **Triggered** [Stackable]
  You cannot manually activate this Effect. Instead, choose a triggering event, always outside your control, that activates this Effect without requiring an Action or Exertion cost from you.
  *Errata: The triggering event must be outside of your control, and balance must be strictly enforced by World Leaders. Suitable events may include: suffering an Injury Severity two or higher, being grappled, falling unconscious, being tied up and bound, being angrily screamed at, etc.*
  Detail required: Triggering Event

- **Waste Not, Want Not** [Trap]
  If disarmed, this consumable trap may be collected and reused.
  *Errata: Triggered traps cannot be disarmed and recollected.*
  Detail required: Disarming Roll

- **Weakness**
  Determine any major weaknesses that the target might have.
  *Errata: Must be things that are systemed around. For example, silver for a werewolf. Not general like "she's bad at fighting".*

- **You have a Sister. . .**
  You learn details about the target's family.
  *Errata: Low Outcome gives info like number of siblings. High Outcome can give names, a random detail about their routines, etc.*

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Targeted, Trap | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Conversationalist**
  You must actively converse with the individual in question for one minute in order to gather any information.

- **Critical Failure**
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Cursed Item** [Trap]
  This consumable is an object that can be armed as a trap. The trap's trigger is using the object instead of entering the proximity of the trap. Ideal for cursed books, trapped phones, etc.
  *Errata: The trap effect's range is still measured from the object itself. The trigger must be an Action, not merely a glance (if the object is a mirror, the target must spend an action looking into the mirror)*

- **Disfiguring** [Targeted, Trap | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Disturbing** [Targeted]
  Witnessing this Effect's Activation is extremely disturbing and/or morally abhorrent to the average person. Anyone who witnesses its activation will almost certainly be repulsed, outraged, and think differently of you from that moment on.
  *Errata: Must go beyond the Effect's innate disturbing-ness. Examples include: transforming into a terrible monster, sacrificing a child, performing torture, etc. Playgroup leaders should account for what is "normal" in their chosen setting. Be cognizant of your group's tone and accommodating to those who would like to "fast forward" through the description.*
  Detail required: Disturbing aspect
  Requires: drawbacks: overt, pact-of-power

- **Easy to Resist**
  The target must either consent to be targeted or be unconscious, hypnotized, thoroughly drugged, in surgery or similarly incapacitated such that they could not or would not roll to resist this Power.
  *Errata: If this condition is satisfied, no defensive rolls may be made by the target.*

- **Exchange of Information**
  For any information you learn about your target, the corresponding information about you is shared with them.

- **Exhausting**
  After you finish activating this Effect, you cannot move quicker than a walk (max 15 feet per Round) for one minute and suffer a -1 dice penalty for an hour. Exhaustion’s penalty and duration stacks with multiple activations.
  *Errata: Exhaustion penalties and duration stack. If you activate this Effect or another Effect with Exhaustion, your penalty will worsen, and the duration is increased by one hour.*

- **Fickle Traps** [Trap]
  Your traps last only an hour once placed.

- **Focus** [Targeted, Trap]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Focused Investigation**
  You learn only one type of information about the target per use.
  *Errata: To take this Drawback, this Power must be capable of revealing more than one type of information.*

- **Fuel** [Targeted, Trap]
  Using this Effect consumes some material good in addition to its other costs.
  Detail required: Fuel Unit

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Mental Resistance**
  The target may resist this Effect by rolling Mind at Difficulty 7.

- **Once per Day** [Targeted, Trap]
  You may only use this Effect once per day.

- **Overt**
  This Effect is obviously Alien and eye-catching across its range to those witnessing it, and you are obviously the source.

- **Physical Resistance**
  The target may resist this Effect by rolling Body at Difficulty 7.

- **Sabotage Item** [Trap]
  Instead of placing a trap at a fixed location, you may sabotage an object that can be carried or moved. The trap's trigger is using the object instead of entering the proximity of the trap. Ideal for poisons, trapping vehicles, cursing clothes, etc.
  *Errata: The trap effect's range is still measured from the object itself. The trigger must be an Action, not merely a glance (if the object is a mirror, the target must spend an action looking into the mirror)*

- **Sacrifice** [Targeted, Trap]
  Instead of Exerting your Mind or expending Source, activating this Effect gives a Severity-1 Injury that worsens by 1 Severity each time you use an Effect with Sacrifice. This Injury cannot be prevented by any means or healed before 1 day has passed.
  *Errata: This Injury does not require Stabilization or cause Battle Scars when it becomes Severe. It cannot be transferred using Energy Transfer.*

- **Secrets Spoken**
  The information you gather is made public. The target and those around will know that it has been revealed. Usually this means the target speaks the information aloud, but other flavors are possible.
  Detail required: Method of Reveal

- **Traumatic** [Targeted, Trap]
  You must make a Trauma roll when you use this Effect. Its Difficulty cannot be reduced by any means. If you fail or Botch, you receive one Mind Damage and a new Trauma.
