# Container of Holding

*Carry lots of stuff in a small container*

**Category:** Extraordinary Object

**Vectors:** Functional

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You possess a container that has an unnaturally large amount of room. Furthermore, the objects inside weigh less.  If the container is torn open, the effect ends, causing the objects to spill out. This Power is not obviously supernatural.

## Errata

You must choose the container when you take this power. If lost or stolen, you must re-take this power to regain the effect.

## Enhancements

- **Convenient Carry-On**
  Your container no longer has increased storage capacity, but it can be collapsed down to the size of a wallet without losing any internal storage space. Collapsing or expanding it costs a Quick Action.
  *Errata: Collapsed Appearance cannot be smaller than a fanny pack.*
  Detail required: Collapsed Appearance

- **Expanded Pocket Size** [Seasoned]
  Your collapsible storage container now has 3x the expected internal storage capacity.
  Requires: enhancements: convenient-carry-on

- **Flexible**
  On a Downtime, you may change this Artifact from one type of container to another. Maximum once per Downtime.
  *Errata: You must have access to your current container at the time of the change. You cannot obtain more than one container through this enhancement.*

- **Locked**
  If anyone other than you opens the container, it appears empty. Others can still destroy it to get at the contents.

- **Purpose-built**
  Your container’s size multiplier is multiplied by 5, but it can only store one very specific type of Object or material.
  *Errata: For example: water, money, animals, gasoline, dirt, etc.*
  Detail required: Chosen Object or Material

- **Suitcase Prison**
  Any Animate being placed inside your container cannot break free.
  *Errata: They may still attempt to slip out when you open your container, and if they have a Gift that would help them get out (e.g. Destroy Object or Intangibility), they may still use those.*
  Requires: enhancements: locked

- **Temporal Bubble** [Seasoned]
  Time is frozen inside your container.
  Requires: enhancements: suitcase-prison

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Functional | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Conservation of Mass**
  The objects you store in your container have their normal weight.

- **Disfiguring** [Functional | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **It's Alive!** [Functional]
  This item is a living thing. When targeted, it counts as a Living Creature in addition to its other target types. If destroyed or abandoned for more than two days, it dies and becomes unusable.

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma
