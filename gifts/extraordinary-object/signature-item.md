# Signature Item

*Protect an important Artifact*

**Category:** Extraordinary Object

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You  have a signature item which is difficult to separate from you. If you do manage to lose it, you have a way of tracking it down.

NOTE: This Power can only be applied to items you already possess, such as those granted by other Gifts.

## Enhancements

- **Burn the Unworthy**
  There is a specific type of being that cannot hold or use this Artifact. They take a Severity 1 Injury each Round they touch or possess of the Artifact. It cannot be prevented or healed by any means until one day after they relinquish possession.
  *Errata: For example, the undead, murderers, religious people, non-humans, etc. If the true owner ever falls into these categories, they cease to be the Artifact’s true owner.*
  Detail required: Type of Being

- **DNA lock** [Seasoned]
  Anyone but this Artifact's true owner must Exert their Mind to wear this Artifact or activate any of its Effects.

- **Homing Beacon**
  This Artifact's true owner is always aware of the direction and distance to this Artifact.

- **Next of Kin**
  If the true owner of this Artifact ever dies, it immediately disappears. At least one week later, it will appear to a qualifying next of kin and reveal its power to them. They become its new true owner.
  *Errata: Qualification cannot name any specific individual or the Artifact’s creator. Examples include: a British child of upstanding moral quality, a descendant of the royal bloodline, or someone who once saved the last true owner’s life. If there is no available Next of Kin, it will wait two months and then appear to a random person. If multiple next of kin are appropriate, the GM will decide randomly.*
  Detail required: Qualification

- **Palantir**
  If this Artifact is used by someone other than its true owner, its true owner is alerted and learns the user's appearance, direction, and distance at that moment. The true owner may use a single investigation Effect on them once at any range.

- **Recall**
  If this Artifact is in no one else’s possession, its true owner may Exert their Mind and spend two Actions to bring it to themselves.
  *Errata: "Possession" means that someone else has claimed the Artifact and has it on their person or stored it in an area they control.*

- **Tether**
  Whenever this Artifact is unattended and within 300 feet of its true owner, it will fly back to them. When not held or stowed, it floats beside its true owner, moving with them. While within 10 feet, the true owner does not need to touch it to use it
  *Errata: The Artifact can fly at a maximum speed of 100 feet per Round and only towards its true owner. This does not grant the Artifact its own Action or Reaction, and it moves at the same time as its true owner’s Movement.*

## Drawbacks

- **Codependence**
  This Artifact's true owner must have it with them to have restful sleep. They cannot regain Mind or Source while separated from this Artifact.

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Focus** [Passive]
  Using this Effect requires a specific kind of item.
  *Errata: Subject to Playgroup Leader approval. Drawing an accessible object (such as one in a holster) counts as a Quick Action (-2 dice), and removing something from a backpack or luggage requires a Full Action.*
  Detail required: Object

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
