# Thrown Weapon

*Shurikens, axes, javelins, and more*

**Category:** Extraordinary Object

**Vectors:** Functional

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Legendary Artifact

## Description

.

## Enhancements

- **Accursed Wounds**
  Any Injury inflicted is considered Unstabilized no matter its Severity. The roll required to Stabilize it is treated as though it is a minimum of Severity 6. These wounds do not heal naturally, though Effects may still be used to heal them.

- **Bane**
  When this weapon injures a specific type of being, the Injury it causes is increased by 2 Severity levels
  Detail required: Type of being

- **Chain Lightning**
  You may Exert your Mind to perform a chain lightning attack. If your attack succeeds, All targets within 5 feet of your primary target take full Damage. The Damage can “jump” up to 5 times. Each target can be hit only once.

- **Compact** [Functional]
  This item can collapse to the size of a lighter. Collapsing or expanding it costs a Quick Action. You cannot expand this Object as an attack.
  *Errata: If there is not enough free space to expand the object, it cannot be expanded.*
  Detail required: Collapsed Appearance

- **Hail Storm**
  You may Exert your Mind to make an attack against all targets within a 120 degree arc in front of you. Your full attack Outcome applies to each of them, but they may attempt to Dodge or Defend if they are aware of the attack.
  Requires: enhancements: magic-quiver

- **Heavy Toss**
  If your attack hits a target, it either knocks them down, or knocks them back 5 feet times the attack's Outcome. Decide which before you roll.

- **Magic Quiver**
  Instead of throwing this Artifact, it is an object that produces an endless supply of temporary throwable objects. For example, bracers that allow you to throw ethereal javelins, or a sack of throwing rocks that doesn’t shrink when you withdraw one.
  *Errata: Created projectiles last only until they strike their target and cannot be shared or stockpiled.*

- **Not Quite Flying**
  You may Exert your Mind and use up your Movement to “follow” any attack using this weapon, traveling right next to its point of impact
  *Errata: The attack must be the primary form of attack for this weapon.*

- **Pin**
  If one of your Attacks injures its target, you may leave your weapon and pin them in place. Safely removing the weapon costs an Action. If they move before removing it, the Injury’s Severity worsens by 2. Using a Quick Action worsens it by 1.
  *Errata: You cannot worsen the Injury by removing the weapon yourself until the following Round.*
  Requires: enhancements: return

- **Pincushion** [Seasoned]
  Any attack or Effect activation roll against a target with an Injury dealt by this weapon is rolled at -1 Difficulty.
  Requires: enhancements: magic-quiver

- **Remote Control** [Seasoned]
  You may choose to deploy this Artifact in drone mode. Each Round it is deployed, instead of attacking, you may spend a Quick Action to have it attack a target with 8 dice. You may end drone mode as a Quick Action on your initiative.
  *Errata: This weapon may Attack up to once per Round.*
  Requires: enhancements: return

- **Return**
  When you throw this weapon, you may opt to have it return to your possession at the end of the attack. Whoever threw this artifact last may use an Action to recall it from up to 100 feet away, attacking one target in its return path as they do so.

- **Ricochet**
  You can make a thrown attack against a target behind cover so long as there is at least one exposed pathway to them.

- **Shredder**
  If you successfully hit your target, the value of any Armor they are wearing is reduced by 2. For material armor, this penalty lasts until it is repaired. For intrinsic armor, it lasts until the end of Combat and any relevant wound is healed.

- **Straight To The Face**
  You may make a throwing attack at melee range with no penalty.

- **Virtuoso's Vessel** [Functional]
  When using this Device for its primary purpose, you always roll a minimum of 8 dice. Excludes Effect activation rolls.
  *Errata: For example, Artifact Weapons would grant the bonus for Attacking, Defending, or Clashing. Artifact vehicles would grant the bonus for driving.*

## Drawbacks

- **Buster** [Functional]
  This item is large, flamboyant, and impossible to conceal. It weighs the same as its mundane counterpart but is at least twice as large.
  *Errata: This does not offer Difficulty penalties for its actual use. The Artifact cannot be broken down for storage and re-assembled. This Artifact cannot be disguised or morphed with other Effects like Shape-Changing object.*

- **Conditional** [Functional | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Disfiguring** [Functional | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **It's Alive!** [Functional]
  This item is a living thing. When targeted, it counts as a Living Creature in addition to its other target types. If destroyed or abandoned for more than two days, it dies and becomes unusable.

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma
