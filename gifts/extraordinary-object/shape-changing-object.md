# Shape-Changing Object

*A morphable item*

**Category:** Extraordinary Object

**Vectors:** Functional

**Modalities:** Artifact Crafting, Legendary Artifact

## Description

.

## Enhancements

- **Compact** [Functional]
  This item can collapse to the size of a lighter. Collapsing or expanding it costs a Quick Action. You cannot expand this Object as an attack.
  *Errata: If there is not enough free space to expand the object, it cannot be expanded.*
  Detail required: Collapsed Appearance

- **Easy**
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Flawless Forgery**
  Your Artifact may now mimic specific objects, such as a particular work of art, family heirlooms, signed papers, etc. It may function as credentials when checked in computer systems.

- **Judge Me By My Size, Do You?** [Seasoned]
  Your Artifact's new appearance may now be a different size, shape, or weight than the Artifact itself.
  *Errata: Interacting with an item that has significantly altered shape, size, or weight may give people a Perception check to notice what's wrong, at GM's discretion.*

- **Multi-tool**
  When your Artifact transforms, you may choose for it to transform into a spliced Object as large as the largest Object it could turn into with the functionality of all the Objects it could turn into.
  *Errata: The resulting object behaves exactly as if all objects occupy the same space and does not gain any new functionality at all.*
  Requires: enhancements: true-change

- **True Change** [Stackable]
  The Artifact makes a genuine transformation into a new item. It is no longer illusory, and it gains whatever functionality the new appearance would have. However, it may only transform into one specific item for each application of this enhancement.
  *Errata: You must be able to acquire this item normally. It can’t be something like an atom bomb or a nuclear submarine.*
  Detail required: New Object

## Drawbacks

- **Conditional** [Functional | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Disfiguring** [Functional | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Illusive Specialist**
  Your Artifact may only take on the appearance of a specific category of Objects.
  *Errata: Category suggestions: Stolen items, rocks, guns, swords, papers, apparel, art, disgusting or ugly things, expensive items, etc.*
  Detail required: Chosen Category

- **Maddening** [Artifact Crafting | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Sacrifice**
  Instead of Exerting your Mind or expending Source, activating this Effect gives a Severity-1 Injury that worsens by 1 Severity each time you use an Effect with Sacrifice. This Injury cannot be prevented by any means or healed before 1 day has passed.
  *Errata: This Injury does not require Stabilization or cause Battle Scars when it becomes Severe. It cannot be transferred using Energy Transfer.*

- **Traumatic**
  You must make a Trauma roll when you use this Effect. Its Difficulty cannot be reduced by any means. If you fail or Botch, you receive one Mind Damage and a new Trauma.
