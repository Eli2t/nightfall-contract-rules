# Mend Trauma

*Heal mental scars and instabilities*

**Category:** Health

**Vectors:** Targeted

**Modalities:** Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

You are capable of treating mental health issues with incredible effectiveness. Treatment takes some time, but you gain an understanding of the source and nature of your patient's traumas as you work.

## Enhancements

- **Battery-operated** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Instead of Exerting Mind to activate this Effect, it has its own 3-point Source pool. The Source regenerates at the rate of one point per day and cannot be raised with Experience.
  *Errata: Different Effects on the same Artifact with this Enhancement share a battery pool. Each additional Battery-Operated after the first increases the capacity of the Source by 3, to a maximum of 12.*

- **Easy** [Targeted]
  When you activate this Effect, flip a coin. If you win, its Exertion cost is refunded.

- **Group Therapy**
  You may target a number of individuals equal to your Charisma. Any Traumas you treat are revealed to the entire group.
  *Errata: This Enhancement can be used with the Enhancement Suppress, but not with the Enhancement Stabilize.*

- **Institutionalize**
  Your target does not need to consent to your treatment. They may choose to contest your efforts by rolling Mind at Difficulty 7.

- **Mechanic**
  This Effect can be used on any Animate targets

- **No Lost Causes** [Veteran]
  Instead of removing a Trauma, you may remove a mental Condition from your target.

- **Stabilize**
  Once per day per target, you may choose to use this Effect to stabilize a target's Mind. Treatment takes 30 seconds but will not remove any Traumas. If you are successful, restore 2 Mind damage.
  *Errata: Using this Power in this way does not cure Traumas.*

- **Strict Regimen**
  Upon treatment, you may specify a clearly-defined behavior that the patient must adhere to. If they violate the rule, the treatment is immediately reversed. Lasts until the end of their next Contract or 1 month for non-Contractors.

- **Suppress**
  You may choose to use this Effect to temporarily relieve any number of the patient's Traumas. In this case, Treatment takes one Action and the effect lasts two hours. If the Patient is currently experiencing an episode, Difficulty is increased by 2.
  *Errata: Using this Effect in this way does not cure Traumas. You may use this to suppress permanent Traumas which were recorded as Conditions.*

- **Sure-Fire** [Seasoned]
  Does not require a roll to activate and will always succeed, barring outside interference.

- **Teletherapy**
  You may now provide therapy at any distance, provided you are able to regularly communicate with your target. The Enhancement Stabilize can be used on a target up to 50 feet away.

## Drawbacks

- **Adjust Trauma**
  If you would successfully remove someone's Trauma, you must instead replace it with a new Trauma.
  Detail required: New Trauma

- **After-care Instructions**
  All of your treatments place the same, specific restriction on the patient. The restriction lasts a month (until the end of their next Contract for Contractors), and if they ever break it, the treatment is immediately reversed.
  *Errata: This restriction should be a significant negative. For example: "the patient cannot travel more than 45 Mph," or "The patient cannot be soaked in water." 

The patient is aware of this requirement.*
  Detail required: Aftercare restriction

- **Conditional** [Targeted | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure**
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Targeted | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Disturbing** [Targeted]
  Witnessing this Effect's Activation is extremely disturbing and/or morally abhorrent to the average person. Anyone who witnesses its activation will almost certainly be repulsed, outraged, and think differently of you from that moment on.
  *Errata: Must go beyond the Effect's innate disturbing-ness. Examples include: transforming into a terrible monster, sacrificing a child, performing torture, etc. Playgroup leaders should account for what is "normal" in their chosen setting. Be cognizant of your group's tone and accommodating to those who would like to "fast forward" through the description.*
  Detail required: Disturbing aspect
  Requires: drawbacks: overt, pact-of-power

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Narrow Expertise**
  You may only cure a certain specific type of Trauma; for example, Phobias, Delusions, or Compulsions.
  Detail required: Type of Trauma

- **Selfish**
  You may only target yourself.

- **Selfless**
  You cannot target yourself.

- **Traumatizing**
  Your target must make a Trauma roll to reap the benefits of this Effect. They may choose to Resist the Effect and not make the roll.
