# Regeneration

*Recover quickly and miraculously*

**Category:** Health | **Required Status:** Novice

**Vectors:** Passive

**Modalities:** Power, Legendary Artifact

## Description

If you are stabilized, your wounds heal at an accelerated rate. This power does not heal disfigurements that a human would not be able to naturally heal given enough time.

## Enhancements

- **Clotting**
  You may Exert your Mind and spend an Action to reduce the Severity of one of your Injuries by 2. Does not affect Injuries suffered as an activation cost for Effects.

- **Healing Surge** [Seasoned]
  You may Exert your Mind and spend a Quick Action to reduce the Severity of one of your Injuries by 4. Does not affect Injuries suffered as an activation cost for Effects.
  Requires: enhancements: clotting

- **Immortal Flesh** [Veteran]
  If you die, you may fully regenerate your body as long as you are in a habitable environment and a piece the size of your hand remains. Regenerating from death takes one month.
  *Errata: While regenerating from death, you cannot take any Actions or use Effects.*
  Requires: enhancements: not-dead-yet

- **Not Dead Yet!** [Seasoned]
  As long as at least half your body remains in tact, you remain at Incapacitated instead of dying, regardless of wound level. You are clearly alive in this state.
  *Errata: This does not prevent you from taking the Injuries, it just means that being Injured enough to take you beyond Incapacitated will not kill you.*

- **Regrowth**
  All Injuries you receive are considered Properly Stabilized. You may also choose to heal any Battle Scar on yourself that may not have been caused by an Injury. Doing so takes one day, and the Battle Scar affects you fully until that time.

## Drawbacks

- **Action Hero**
  Your Regeneration cannot reduce any given Injury's Severity by more than two levels total.
  *Errata: After the first two levels, healing occurs at a normal rate.*

- **Conditional** [Passive | Stackable]
  This Effect is only available when a certain condition is met. This condition must be severe enough that this Gift is unusable the majority of the time.
  *Errata: The condition must be significant. Subject to GM approval.*
  Detail required: Condition

- **Critical Failure** [Passive]
  Roll a single D10 when you activate this Effect.  If you roll a 1, you critically fail in a way that is extraordinarily dangerous. Failure should cause at least a Severity-4 Injury, a Major Battle Scar, or an Effect counter to its purpose.
  *Errata: Other examples: You are incapacitated for 5 minutes, you instead activate a different Effect counter to this one (i.e. Blast instead of Fascinate), you randomly teleport within 50 feet radius of your position (GM chooses the worst position), you cannot use other activated/targeted Effects for 1 hour, etc.*
  Detail required: Critical Failure effect

- **Disfiguring** [Passive | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift grants a minor Battle Scar that can only be cured by buying off this Drawback.
  *Errata: E.g. chronic pain, wheezing, disfigurement, or a change in appearance that makes you obviously inhuman or similarly outlandish for settings where non-humans are common. See the stock Battle Scars on your character sheet for system references.*
  Detail required: Scar

- **Maddening** [Power | Stackable]
  Possession of this Gift causes a Trauma that cannot be cured by any means. The chosen Trauma must be likely to come up on a Contract.
  *Errata: Buying off this Drawback cures the Trauma. The Trauma should require a Self-Control roll to avoid serious issues and loss of control. For example, "you must roll Self-Control to surface from any body of water," or "you must roll Self-Control to avoid jumping into fire when you see it," or "you cannot trust your life to someone else without rolling Self-Control." etc.*
  Detail required: Trauma

- **Silver Bullets**
  Injuries from a particular type of material, attack, or source of damage are not affected by your regeneration, and have their Severity increased by 1.
  *Errata: e.g. Silver, fire, blows to the head, blunt weapons, etc.*
  Detail required: Weakness

- **Unreliable** [Passive]
  When you activate this Effect, roll a single d10, Difficulty 4. If you fail or botch, the Effect fails and you cannot activate it again for an hour.
  *Errata: Any activation costs must still be paid.*
